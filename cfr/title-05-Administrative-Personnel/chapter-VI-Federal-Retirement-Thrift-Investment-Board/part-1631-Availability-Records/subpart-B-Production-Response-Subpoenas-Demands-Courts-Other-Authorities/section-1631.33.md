##### ยง 1631.33 Procedure in the event of an adverse ruling. #####

If the court or other authority declines to stay the effect of the demand in response to a request made in accordance with ยง 1631.32(b) pending receipt of instructions from the Executive Director, or his or her designee, or if the court or other authority rules that the demand must be complied with irrespective of the instructions from the Executive Director not to produce the material or disclose the information sought, the employee or former employee upon whom the demand has been made shall respectfully decline to comply with the demand.
