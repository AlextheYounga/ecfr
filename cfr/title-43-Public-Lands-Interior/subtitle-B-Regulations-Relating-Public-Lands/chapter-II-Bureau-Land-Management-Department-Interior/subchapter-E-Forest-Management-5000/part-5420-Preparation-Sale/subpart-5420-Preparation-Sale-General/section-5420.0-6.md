##### ยง 5420.0-6 Policy. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 82374, Dec. 18, 2020.

All timber or other vegetative resources to be sold shall be appraised and in no case shall be sold at less than the appraised value. Measurement shall be by tree cruise, log scale, weight, or such other form of measurement as may be determined to be in the public interest.
