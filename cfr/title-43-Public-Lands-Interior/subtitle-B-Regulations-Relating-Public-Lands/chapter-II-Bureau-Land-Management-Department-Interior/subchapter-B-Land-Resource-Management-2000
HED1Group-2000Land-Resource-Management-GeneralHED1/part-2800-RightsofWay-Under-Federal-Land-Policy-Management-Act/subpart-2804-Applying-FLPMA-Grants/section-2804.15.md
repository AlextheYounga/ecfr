##### ยง 2804.15 When does BLM reevaluate the processing and monitoring fees? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 25960, Apr. 12, 2024.

BLM reevaluates the processing and monitoring fees (*see* ยง 2805.16 of this part) for each category and the categories themselves within 5 years after they go into effect and at 10-year intervals after that. When reevaluating processing and monitoring fees, BLM considers all factors that affect the fees, including, but not limited to, any changes in:

(a) Technology;

(b) The procedures for processing applications and monitoring grants;

(c) Statutes and regulations relating to the right-of-way program; or

(d) The IPD-GDP.
