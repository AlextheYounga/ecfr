##### ยง 4.1131 Time for discovery. #####

Link to an amendment published at 90 FR 2430, Jan. 10, 2025.This amendment is delayed until March 21, 2025, at 90 FR 9222, Feb. 10, 2025.

Following the initiation of a proceeding, the parties may initiate discovery at any time as long as it does not interfere with the conduct of the hearing.
