##### ยง 64.6030 Inmate Calling Services interim rate cap. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 40731, July 28, 2021.

No provider shall charge a rate for interstate Collect Calling in excess of $0.25 per minute, or a rate for interstate Debit Calling, Prepaid Calling, or Prepaid Collect Calling in excess of $0.21 per minute. These interim rate caps shall remain in effect until permanent rate caps are adopted and take effect.
