##### ยง 64.630 Applicability of change of default TRS provider rules. #####

Link to an amendment published at 82 FR 17764, Apr. 13, 2017.

Sections 64.630 through 64.636 of this part governing changes in default TRS providers shall apply to any provider of IP Relay or VRS eligible to receive payments from the TRS Fund.
