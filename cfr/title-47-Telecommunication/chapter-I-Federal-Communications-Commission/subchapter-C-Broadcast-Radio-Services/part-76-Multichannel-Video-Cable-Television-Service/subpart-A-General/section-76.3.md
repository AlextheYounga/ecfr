##### § 76.3 Other pertinent rules. #####

Other pertinent provisions of the Commission's rules and regulations relating to Multichannel Video and the Cable Television Service are included in the following parts of this chapter:

Part 1—Practice and Procedure.Part 11—Emergency Alert System (EAS).Part 21—Domestic Public Radio Services (Other Than Maritime Mobile).Part 63—Extension of Lines and Discontinuance of Service by Carriers.Part 64—Miscellaneous Rules Relating to Common Carriers.Part 78—Cable Television Relay Service.Part 79—Closed Captioning of Video Programming.Part 91—Industrial Radio Services.[65 FR 53614, Sept. 5, 2000]
