##### ยง 0.475 Applications for employment. #####

Persons who wish to apply for employment should communicate with the Associate Managing Director-Personnel Management.

(Secs. 4(i), 303(n), Communications Act of 1934, as amended, 47 U.S.C. 154(i) and 303(n); 47 CFR 0.231(d)) [49 FR 13368, Apr. 4, 1984]
