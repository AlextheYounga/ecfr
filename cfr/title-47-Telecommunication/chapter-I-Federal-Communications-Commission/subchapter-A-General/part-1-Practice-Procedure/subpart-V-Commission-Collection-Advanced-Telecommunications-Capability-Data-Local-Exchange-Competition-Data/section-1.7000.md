##### ยง 1.7000 Purpose. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 50907, Aug. 18, 2020.

The purposes of this subpart are to set out the terms by which certain commercial and government-controlled entities report data to the Commission concerning (a) the provision of wired and wireless local telephone services and interconnected Voice over internet Protocol services, and (b) the deployment of advanced telecommunications capability, as defined in 47 U.S.C. 1302, and services that are competitive with advanced telecommunications capability.
