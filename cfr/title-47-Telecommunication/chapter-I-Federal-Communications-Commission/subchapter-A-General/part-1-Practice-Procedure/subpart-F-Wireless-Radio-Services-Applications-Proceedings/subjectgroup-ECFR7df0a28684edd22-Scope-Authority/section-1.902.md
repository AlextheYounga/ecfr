##### ยง 1.902 Scope. #####

In case of any conflict between the rules set forth in this subpart and the rules set forth in parts 13, 20, 22, 24, 26, 27, 74, 80, 87, 90, 95, 96, 97, and 101 of this chapter, the rules in this subpart shall govern.
