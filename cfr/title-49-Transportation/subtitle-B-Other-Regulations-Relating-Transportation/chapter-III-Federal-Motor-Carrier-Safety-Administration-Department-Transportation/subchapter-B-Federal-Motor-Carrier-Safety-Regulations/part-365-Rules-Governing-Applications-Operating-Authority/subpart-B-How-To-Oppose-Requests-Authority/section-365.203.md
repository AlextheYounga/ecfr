##### ยง 365.203 Time for filing. #####

A protest shall be filed (received at the FMCSA) within 10 days after notice of the application appears in the *FMCSA Register.* A copy of the protest shall be sent to applicant's representative at the same time. Failure timely to file a protest waives further participation in the proceeding.
