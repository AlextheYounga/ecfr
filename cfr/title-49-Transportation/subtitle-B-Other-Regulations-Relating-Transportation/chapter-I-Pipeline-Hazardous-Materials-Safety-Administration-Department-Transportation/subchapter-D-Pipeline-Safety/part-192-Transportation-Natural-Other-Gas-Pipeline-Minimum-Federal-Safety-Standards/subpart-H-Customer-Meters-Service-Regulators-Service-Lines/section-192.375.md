##### ยง 192.375 Service lines: Plastic. #####

(a) Each plastic service line outside a building must be installed below ground level, except that -

(1) It may be installed in accordance with ยง 192.321(g); and

(2) It may terminate above ground level and outside the building, if -

(i) The above ground level part of the plastic service line is protected against deterioration and external damage; and

(ii) The plastic service line is not used to support external loads.

(b) Each plastic service line inside a building must be protected against external damage.
