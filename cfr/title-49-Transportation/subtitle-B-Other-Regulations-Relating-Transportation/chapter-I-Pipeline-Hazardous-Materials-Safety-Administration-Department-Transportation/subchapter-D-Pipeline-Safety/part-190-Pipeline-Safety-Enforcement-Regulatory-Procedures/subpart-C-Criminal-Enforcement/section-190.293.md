##### ยง 190.293 Referral for prosecution. #####

If a PHMSA employee becomes aware of any actual or possible activity subject to criminal penalties under ยง 190.291, the employee reports it to the Office of Chief Counsel, Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration, and to his or her supervisor.
