##### ยง 106.105 PHMSA response to a petition for rulemaking. #####

We will review and respond to your petition for rulemaking as follows:

|If your petition is . . .|    And if we  <br/>determine  <br/>that . . .    |                                 Then . . .                                  |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|     (a) Incomplete      |                                                  |           We may return your petition with a written explanation.           |
|      (b) Complete       |Your petition does not justify a rulemaking action|We will notify you in writing that we will not start a rulemaking proceeding.|
|      (c) Complete       |  Your petition does justify a rulemaking action  |  We will notify you in writing that we will start a rulemaking proceeding.  |
