##### ยง 75.650 Participation of students enrolled in private schools. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 70333, Aug. 29, 2024.

If the authorizing statute for a program requires a grantee to provide for participation by students enrolled in private schools, the grantee shall provide a genuine opportunity for equitable participation in accordance with the requirements that apply to subgrantees under 34 CFR 76.650-76.662.

(Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
