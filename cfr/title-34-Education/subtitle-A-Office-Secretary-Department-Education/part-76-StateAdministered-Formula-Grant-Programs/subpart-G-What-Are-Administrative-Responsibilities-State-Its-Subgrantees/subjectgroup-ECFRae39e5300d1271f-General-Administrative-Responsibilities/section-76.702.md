##### ยง 76.702 Fiscal control and fund accounting procedures. #####

A State and a subgrantee shall use fiscal control and fund accounting procedures that ensure proper disbursement of and accounting for Federal funds.

(Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3, 3474, and 6511(a)) [45 FR 22517, Apr. 3, 1980, as amended at 89 FR 70339, Aug. 29, 2024]
