##### ยง 76.761 Federal funds may pay 100 percent of cost. #####

A State or a subgrantee may use program funds to pay up to 100 percent of the cost of a project if:

(a) The State or subgrantee is not required to match the funds; and

(b) The project can be assisted under applicable statutes and regulations.

(Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3, 3474, and 6511(a)) [45 FR 22517, Apr. 3, 1980, as amended at 89 FR 70340, Aug. 29, 2024]
