##### ยง 350.13 What must a grantee do in carrying out a research activity? #####

In carrying out a research activity under this program, a grantee shall -

(a) Identify one or more hypotheses; and

(b) Based on the hypotheses identified, perform an intensive systematic study directed toward -

(1) New or full scientific knowledge; or

(2) Understanding of the subject or problem studied.

(Authority: Sec. 202; 29 U.S.C. 761a)
