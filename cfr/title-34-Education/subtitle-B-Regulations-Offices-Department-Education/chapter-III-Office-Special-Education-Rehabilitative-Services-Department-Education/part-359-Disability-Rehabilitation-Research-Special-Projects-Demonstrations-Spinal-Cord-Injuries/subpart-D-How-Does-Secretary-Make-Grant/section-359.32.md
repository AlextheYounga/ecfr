##### ยง 359.32 What additional factors does the Secretary consider in making a grant under this program? #####

In determining which applicants to fund under this program, the Secretary also considers the proposed location of any project in order to achieve, to the extent possible, a geographic distribution of projects.

(Authority: Sec. 204(b)(4)(C); 29 U.S.C. 762(b)(4)(C))[52 FR 30066, Aug. 12, 1987]
