##### ยง 365.31 What are the standards for service providers? #####

(a) The DSU shall develop, establish, make available to the public, maintain, and implement written minimum standards for the provision of -

(1) IL services to be met by service providers that are not centers; and

(2) Specialized IL services to individuals with significant disabilities by centers under a contract with the DSU.

(b) The minimum standards developed pursuant to paragraph (a)(2) of this section may differ from the standards and assurances in section 725 of the Act and subparts F and G of 34 CFR part 366.

(c) The DSU shall assure that participating service providers meet all applicable State licensure or certification requirements.

(Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527)(Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c))
