##### ยง 365.10 How does a State apply for a grant? #####

To receive a grant under this part, a State shall submit to the Secretary and obtain approval of a State plan that meets the requirements of part A of title VII of the Act and subparts B and C of 34 CFR part 364.

(Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527)(Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796c(a)(1) and (c) and 796e(a))
