##### § 222.32 What information does the Secretary use to determine a local educational agency's basic support payment? #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 64741, September 20, 2016.

(a) The Secretary determines an LEA's payment under section 8003(b) on the basis of information in the LEA's application, including information regarding the membership of federally connected children.

(b) The LEA must supply information in its application regarding its federally connected membership on the basis of any count described in §§ 222.33 through 222.35.

(Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1810-0036)(Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7703 and 7705)
