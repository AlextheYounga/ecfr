##### ยง 402.5 What definitions apply? #####

The following definitions apply to the Native Hawaiian Vocational Education Program:

(a) The definitions in 34 CFR 400.4 apply to this part.

(b) The following definition also applies to this part:

*Native Hawaiian* means any individual who has any ancestors who were natives, prior to 1778, of the area that now comprises the State of Hawaii.

(Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2313(a)(1)(B))
