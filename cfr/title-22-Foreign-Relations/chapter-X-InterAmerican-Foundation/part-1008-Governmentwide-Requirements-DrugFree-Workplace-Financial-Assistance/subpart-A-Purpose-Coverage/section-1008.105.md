##### § 1008.105 Does this part apply to me? #####

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either—

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Inter-American Foundation; or

(2) A(n) Inter-American Foundation awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in §§ 1008.605 and 1008.660, respectively.)

(b) The following table shows the subparts that apply to you:

|                  If you are . . .                  |see subparts . . .|
|----------------------------------------------------|------------------|
|      (1) A recipient who is not an individual      |   A, B and E.    |
|        (2) A recipient who is an individual        |   A, C and E.    |
|(3) A(n) Inter-American Foundation awarding official|   A, D and E.    |
