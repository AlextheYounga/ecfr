##### ยง 585.637 When may I commence commercial operations on my commercial lease? #####

If you are conducting activities on your lease that:

(a) Do not require a FERC license (*i.e.,* wind), then you may commence commercial operations 30 days after the CVA or project engineer has submitted to BOEM the final Fabrication and Installation Report for the fabrication and installation review, as provided in ยง 585.708.

(b) Require a FERC license or exemption, then you may commence commercial operations when permitted by the terms of your license or exemption.
