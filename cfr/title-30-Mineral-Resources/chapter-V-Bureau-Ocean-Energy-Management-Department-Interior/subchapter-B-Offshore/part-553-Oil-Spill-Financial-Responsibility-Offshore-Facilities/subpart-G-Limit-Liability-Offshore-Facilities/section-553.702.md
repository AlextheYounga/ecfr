##### ยง 553.702 What limit of liability applies to my offshore facility? #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 2542, Jan. 18, 2018.

Except as provided in 33 U.S.C. 2704(c), the limit of liability under OPA for a responsible party for any offshore facility, including any offshore pipeline, is the total of all removal costs plus $133.65 million for damages with respect to each incident.
