##### § 585.600 What plans must I submit to BOEM before I conduct activities on my lease or grant? #####

You must submit a SAP, COP, or GAP and receive BOEM approval as set forth in the following table:

|                                                     Before you:                                                      |You must submit and obtain approval for your:|
|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
|                         (a) Conduct any site assessment activities on your commercial lease                          |SAP, according to §§ 585.605 through 585.613.|
|(b) Conduct any activities pertaining to construction of facilities for commercial operations on your commercial lease|COP, according to §§ 585.620 through 585.628.|
|              (c) Conduct any activities on your limited lease, ROW grant, or RUE grant in any OCS area               |GAP, according to §§ 585.640 through 585.648.|
