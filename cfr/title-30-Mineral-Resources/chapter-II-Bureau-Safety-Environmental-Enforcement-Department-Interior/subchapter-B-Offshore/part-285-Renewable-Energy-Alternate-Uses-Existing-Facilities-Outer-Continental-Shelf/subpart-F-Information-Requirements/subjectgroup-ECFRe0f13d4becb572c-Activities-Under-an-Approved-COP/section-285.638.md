##### § 285.638 What must I do upon completion of my commercial operations as approved in my COP or FERC license? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 42715, May 15, 2024.

(a) Upon completion of your approved activities under your COP, you must initiate the decommissioning process as set forth in subpart I of this part. You must submit your decommissioning application as provided in §§ 285.905 and 285.906.

(b) Upon completion of your approved activities under your FERC license, the terms of your FERC license will govern your decommissioning activities.
