##### ยง 285.602 What records must I maintain? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 42715, May 15, 2024.

Until BOEM releases your financial assurance under 30 CFR 585.534, you must maintain and provide to BSEE, upon request, all data and information related to compliance with required terms and conditions of your SAP, COP, or GAP.
