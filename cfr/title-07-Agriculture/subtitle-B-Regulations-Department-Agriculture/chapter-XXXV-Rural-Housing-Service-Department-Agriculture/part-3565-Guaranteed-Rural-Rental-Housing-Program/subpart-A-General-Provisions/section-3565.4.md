##### ยง 3565.4 Availability of assistance. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 55035, Oct. 15, 2019.

The Agency's authority to enter into commitments, guarantee loans, or provide interest credits is limited to the extent that appropriations are available to cover the cost of the assistance. The Agency will publish a NOFA in the Federal Register to notify interested parties of the availability of assistance.
