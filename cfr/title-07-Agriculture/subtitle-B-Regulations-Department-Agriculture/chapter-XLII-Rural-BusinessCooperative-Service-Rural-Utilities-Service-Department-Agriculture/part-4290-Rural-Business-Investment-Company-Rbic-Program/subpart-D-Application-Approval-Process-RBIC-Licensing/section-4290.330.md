##### ยง 4290.330 Grant and guarantee issuance fee. #####

The Applicant must pay to the Secretary an issuance fee for each grant or debenture guarantee of $500. If both a grant and debenture guarantee are issued for the same RBIC, the issuance fee for both is $500. An Applicant must submit this fee in advance, at the time of application submission.
