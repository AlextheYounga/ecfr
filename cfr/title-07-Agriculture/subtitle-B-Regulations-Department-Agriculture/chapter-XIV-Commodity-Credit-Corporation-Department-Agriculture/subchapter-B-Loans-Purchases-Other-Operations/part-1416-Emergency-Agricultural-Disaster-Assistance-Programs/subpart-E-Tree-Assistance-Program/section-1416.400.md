L. 113-79, the 2014 Farm Bill), as amended by the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2018 (Pub. L. 115-123), and the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2018 (Pub. L. 115-141).

(b) Eligible orchardists and nursery tree growers will be compensated as specified in ยง 1416.406 for eligible tree, bush, and vine losses in excess of 15 percent mortality, or, where applicable, damage in excess of 15 percent, adjusted for normal mortality and normal damage, that occurred in the calendar year (or loss period in the case of plant disease) for which benefits are being requested and as a direct result of a natural disaster.

(c) Eligible pecan tree losses incurred in the 2017 calendar year not meeting the mortality loss threshold of paragraph (b) of this section with a tree mortality loss in excess of 7.5 percent (adjusted for normal mortality) will be compensated for eligible losses as specified in ยง 1416.406, up to a maximum of $15,000,000.
