##### ยง 1484.38 Can a Cooperator keep proceeds generated from an activity? #####

Any income or refunds generated from an activity, *i.e.*, participation fees, proceeds of sales, refunds of value added taxes (VAT), the expenditures for which have been wholly or partially reimbursed, shall be repaid by submitting a check payable to Commodity Credit Corporation or by offsetting the Cooperator's next reimbursement claim.
