##### § 801.4 Tolerances for dockage testers. #####

The maintenance tolerances for dockage testers used in performing official inspection services shall be:

|          Item          |                                      Tolerance                                       |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|     Air separation     |±0.10 percent, mean deviation from standard dockage tester using Hard Red Winter wheat|
|   Riddle separation    |±0.10 percent, mean deviation from standard dockage tester using Hard Red Winter wheat|
|    Sieve separation    |±0.10 percent, mean deviation from standard dockage tester using Hard Red Winter wheat|
|Total dockage separation|±0.15 percent, mean deviation from standard dockage tester using Hard Red Winter wheat|
