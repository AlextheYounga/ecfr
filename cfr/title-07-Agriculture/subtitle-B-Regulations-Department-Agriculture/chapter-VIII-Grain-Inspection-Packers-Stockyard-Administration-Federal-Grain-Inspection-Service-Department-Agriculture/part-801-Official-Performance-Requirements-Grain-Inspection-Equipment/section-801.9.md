##### § 801.9 Tolerances for test weight apparatuses. #####

The maintenance tolerances for test weight per bushel apparatuses used in performing official inspection services shall be:

|       Item        |                                      Tolerance                                       |
|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|Beam/scale accuracy|         ±0.10 pound per bushel deviation at any reading, using test weights          |
| Overall accuracy  |±0.15 pound per bushel, mean deviation from standard test weight apparatus using wheat|
