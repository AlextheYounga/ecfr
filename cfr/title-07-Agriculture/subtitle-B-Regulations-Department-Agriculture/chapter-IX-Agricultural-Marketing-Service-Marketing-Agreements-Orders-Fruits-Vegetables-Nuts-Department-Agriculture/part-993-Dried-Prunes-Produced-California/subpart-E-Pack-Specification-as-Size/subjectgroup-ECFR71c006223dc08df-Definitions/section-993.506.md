##### § 993.506 Lot. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 13589, Feb. 23, 2024.

*Lot* for the purposes of this subpart shall have the same meaning as defined in § 993.104(b) of the Subpart—Administrative Rules and Regulations.

Effective Date Note:At 70 FR 30613, May 27, 2005, § 993.506 was suspended indefinitely.
