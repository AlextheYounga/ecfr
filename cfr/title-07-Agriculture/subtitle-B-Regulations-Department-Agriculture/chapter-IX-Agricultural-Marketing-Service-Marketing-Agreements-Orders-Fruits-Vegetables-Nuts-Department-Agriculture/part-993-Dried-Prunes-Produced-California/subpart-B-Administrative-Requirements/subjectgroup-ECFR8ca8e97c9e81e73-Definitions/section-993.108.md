##### ยง 993.108 Non-human consumption outlet. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 13589, Feb. 23, 2024.

*Non-human consumption outlet* means any livestock feeder or manufacturer of inedible syrup, industrial alcohol, animal feed, or other product for non-human use, who has established, to the satisfaction of the committee, that any prunes or prune waste received for a non-human use will be used only within such outlet.
