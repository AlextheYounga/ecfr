##### ยง 930.256 Free and restricted percentages for the 2018-19 crop year. #####

The percentages for tart cherries handled by handlers during the crop year beginning on July 1, 2018, which shall be free and restricted, respectively, are designated as follows: Free percentage, 73 percent and restricted percentage, 27 percent.
