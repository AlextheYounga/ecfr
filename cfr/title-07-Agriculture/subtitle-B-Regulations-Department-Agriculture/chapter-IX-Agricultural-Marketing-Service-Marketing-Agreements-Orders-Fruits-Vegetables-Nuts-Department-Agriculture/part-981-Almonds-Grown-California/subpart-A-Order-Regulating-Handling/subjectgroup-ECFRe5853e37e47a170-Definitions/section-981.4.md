##### ยง 981.4 Almonds. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 37968, May 7, 2024.

*Almonds* means (unless otherwise specified) all varieties of almonds (except bitter almonds), either shelled or unshelled, grown in the State of California, and for the purposes of research includes almond shells and hulls.
