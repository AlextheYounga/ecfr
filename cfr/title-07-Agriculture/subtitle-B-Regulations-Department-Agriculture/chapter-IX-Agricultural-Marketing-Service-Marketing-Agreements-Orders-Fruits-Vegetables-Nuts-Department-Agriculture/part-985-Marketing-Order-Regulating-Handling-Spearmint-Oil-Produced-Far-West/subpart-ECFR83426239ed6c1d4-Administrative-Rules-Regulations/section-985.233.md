##### ยง 985.233 Salable quantities and allotment percentages - 2014-2015 marketing year. #####

The salable quantity and allotment percentage for each class of spearmint oil during the marketing year beginning on June 1, 2014, shall be as follows:

(a) Class 1 (Scotch) oil - a salable quantity of 1,984,423 pounds and an allotment percentage of 95 percent.

(b) Class 3 (Native) oil - a salable quantity of 1,351,704 pounds and an allotment percentage of 57 percent.
