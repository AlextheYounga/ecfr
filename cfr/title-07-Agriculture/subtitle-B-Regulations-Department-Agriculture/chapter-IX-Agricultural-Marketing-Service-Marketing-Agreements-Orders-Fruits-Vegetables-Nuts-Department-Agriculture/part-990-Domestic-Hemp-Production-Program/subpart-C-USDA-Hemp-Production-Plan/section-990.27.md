##### ยง 990.27 Non-compliant cannabis plants. #####

(a) Cannabis plants exceeding the acceptable hemp THC level constitute marijuana, a schedule I controlled substance under the Controlled Substances Act (CSA), 21 U.S.C. 801 *et seq.,* and must be disposed of in accordance with the CSA and DEA regulations found at 21 CFR 1317.15.

(b) Producers must notify USDA of their intent to dispose of non-conforming plants and verify disposal by submitting required documentation.
