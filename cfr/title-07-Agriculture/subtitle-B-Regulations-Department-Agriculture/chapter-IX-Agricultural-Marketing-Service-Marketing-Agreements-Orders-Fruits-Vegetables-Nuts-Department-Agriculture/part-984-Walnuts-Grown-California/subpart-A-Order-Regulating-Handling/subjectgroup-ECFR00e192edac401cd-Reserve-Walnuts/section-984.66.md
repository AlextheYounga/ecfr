##### ยง 984.66 Assistance of the Board in meeting reserve obligation. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 27109, May 7, 2020.

The Board may assist any handler in accounting for his reserve obligation and may aid any handler in acquiring walnuts to meet any deficiency in his reserve obligation, or in accounting for, or disposing of reserve walnuts.
