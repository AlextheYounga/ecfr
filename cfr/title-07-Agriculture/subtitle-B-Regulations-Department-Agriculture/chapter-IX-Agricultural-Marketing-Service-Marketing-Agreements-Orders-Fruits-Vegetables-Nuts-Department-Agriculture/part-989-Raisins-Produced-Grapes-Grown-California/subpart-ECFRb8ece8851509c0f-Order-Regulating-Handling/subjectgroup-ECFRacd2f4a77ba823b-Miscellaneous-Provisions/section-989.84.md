##### ยง 989.84 Disposition limitation. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 53972, Oct. 26, 2018.

No handler shall dispose of free or reserve tonnage raisins, offgrade raisins, or other failing raisins, except in accordance with the provisions of this subpart or pursuant to regulations issued by the committee.
