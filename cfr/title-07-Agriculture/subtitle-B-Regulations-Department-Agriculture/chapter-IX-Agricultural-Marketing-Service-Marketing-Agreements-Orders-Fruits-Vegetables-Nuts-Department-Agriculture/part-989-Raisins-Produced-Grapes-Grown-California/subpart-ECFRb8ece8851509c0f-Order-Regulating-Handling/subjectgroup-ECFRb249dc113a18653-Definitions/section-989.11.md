##### § 989.11 Producer. #####

*Producer* means any person engaged in a proprietary capacity in the production of grapes which are sun-dried or dehydrated by artificial means until they become raisins: *Provided,* That a “producer” shall include any person whose production unit has qualified for diversion under a diversion program announced by the Committee.
