##### ยง 51.58 Equipment. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 106234, Dec. 30, 2024.This amendment was delayed until Mar. 20, 2025, at 90 FR 8367, Jan. 29, 2025.

All equipment used for receiving, washing, grading, packaging or storing shall be of such design, material and construction that it may be kept clean.
