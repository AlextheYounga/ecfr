##### ยง 51.1402 Size classification. #####

Size of pecans may be specified in connection with the grade in accordance with one of the following classifications. To meet the requirements for any one of these classifications, the lot must conform to both the specified number of nuts per pound and the weight of the 10 smallest nuts per 100 nut sample.

|                      Size classification                      |                       Number of nuts per pound                        |                              Minimum weight of the 10 smallest nuts in a 100-nut sample                               |
|---------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|Oversize  <br/>Extra large  <br/>Large  <br/>Medium  <br/>Small|55 or less  <br/>56 to 63  <br/>64 to 77  <br/>78 to 95  <br/>96 to 120|In each classification, the 10 smallest nuts per 100 must weigh at least 7 pct of the total weight of a 100-nut sample.|
