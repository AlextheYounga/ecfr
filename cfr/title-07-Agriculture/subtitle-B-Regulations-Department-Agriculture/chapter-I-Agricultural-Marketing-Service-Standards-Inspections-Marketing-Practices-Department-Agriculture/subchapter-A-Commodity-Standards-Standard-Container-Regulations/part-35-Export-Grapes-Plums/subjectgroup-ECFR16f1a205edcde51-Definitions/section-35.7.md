##### § 35.7 Certificate. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 7444, Feb. 10, 2020.

*Certificate* or *Certificate of Inspection* means any of the official forms of inspection certificate, bearing the statement “meets Export Grape and Plum Act,” issued by the Federal or Federal-State Inspection Service in accordance with regulations governing the inspection of fresh fruits, vegetables, and other products (7 CFR part 28511).

1 Part 2851 was redesignated as part 51 at 46 FR 63203, Dec. 31, 1981.
