##### ยง 1206.32 Term of office. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 11097, Feb. 24, 2021.

The term of office for first handler, importer, domestic producer, and foreign producer and foreign processor members of the Board will be three years. Members may serve a maximum of two consecutive three-year terms. Each term of office will end on December 31, with new terms of office beginning on January 1.
