##### ยง 1206.11 Mangos. #####

*Mangos* means the fruit of *Mangifera indica L.* of the family *Anacardiaceae.* For purposes of this Order, the term mangos includes:

(a) *Fresh mangos,* which means mangos in their fresh form; and

(b) *Frozen mangos,* which means mangos that are uncooked or cooked by steaming or boiling in water, and then frozen, whether or not containing added sugar or other sweetening agent.
