##### ยง 1216.15 Minor peanut-producing states. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 72151, Dec. 21, 2021.

*Minor peanut-producing states* means all peanut-producing states with the exception of Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Mississippi, Missouri, New Mexico, North Carolina, Oklahoma, South Carolina, Texas and Virginia.
