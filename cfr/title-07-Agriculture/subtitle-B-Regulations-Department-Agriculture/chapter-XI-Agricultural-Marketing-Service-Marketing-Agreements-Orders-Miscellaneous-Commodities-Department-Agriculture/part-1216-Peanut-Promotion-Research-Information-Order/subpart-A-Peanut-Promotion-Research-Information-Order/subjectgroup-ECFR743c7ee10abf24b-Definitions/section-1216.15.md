##### ยง 1216.15 Minor peanut-producing states. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 16231, Mar. 23, 2020.

*Minor peanut-producing states* means all peanut-producing states with the exception of Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Mississippi, New Mexico, North Carolina, Oklahoma, South Carolina, Texas, and Virginia.
