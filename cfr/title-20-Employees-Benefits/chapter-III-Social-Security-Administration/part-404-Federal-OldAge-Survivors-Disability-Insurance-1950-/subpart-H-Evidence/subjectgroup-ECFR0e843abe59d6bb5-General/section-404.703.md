##### ยง 404.703 When evidence is needed. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 107258, Dec. 31, 2024.

When you apply for benefits, we will ask for evidence that you are eligible for them. After you become entitled to benefits, we may ask for evidence showing whether you continue to be entitled to benefits; or evidence showing whether your benefit payments should be reduced or stopped.
