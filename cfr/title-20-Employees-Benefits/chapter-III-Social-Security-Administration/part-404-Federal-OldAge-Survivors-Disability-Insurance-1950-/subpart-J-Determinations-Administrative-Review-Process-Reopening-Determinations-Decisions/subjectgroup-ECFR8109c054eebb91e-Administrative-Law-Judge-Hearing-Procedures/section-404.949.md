##### ยง 404.949 Presenting written statements and oral arguments. #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 90993, December 16, 2016.

You or a person you designate to act as your representative may appear before the administrative law judge to state your case, to present a written summary of your case, or to enter written statements about the facts and law material to your case in the record. A copy of your written statements should be filed for each party.
