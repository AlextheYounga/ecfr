##### ยง 404.2118 Applicability of these provisions to alternate participants. #####

When an alternate participant provides rehabilitation services under this subpart, the payment procedures stated herein shall apply except that:

(a) Payment must be consistent with the cost principles described in 45 CFR part 74 or 41 CFR parts 1-15 as appropriate; and

(b) Any disputes, including appeals of audit determinations, shall be resolved in accordance with applicable statutes and regulations which will be specified in the negotiated agreement or contract.
