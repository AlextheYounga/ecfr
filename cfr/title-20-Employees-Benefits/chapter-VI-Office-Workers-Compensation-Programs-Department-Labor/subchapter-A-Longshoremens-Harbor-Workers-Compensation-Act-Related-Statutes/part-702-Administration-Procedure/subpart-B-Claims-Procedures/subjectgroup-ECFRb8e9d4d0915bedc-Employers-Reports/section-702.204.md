Any employer, insurance carrier, or self-insured employer who knowingly and willfully fails or refuses to send any report required by ยง 702.201, or who knowingly or willfully makes a false statement or misrepresentation in any report, shall be subject to a civil penalty not to exceed $22,587 for each such failure, refusal, false statement, or misrepresentation for which penalties are assessed after August 1, 2016.
