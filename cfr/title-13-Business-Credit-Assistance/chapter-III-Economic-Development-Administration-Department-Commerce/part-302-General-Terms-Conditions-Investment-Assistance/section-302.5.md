##### ยง 302.5 Relocation assistance and land acquisition policies. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 8379, Feb. 14, 2020.

Recipients of EDA Investment Assistance or any other types of assistance under PWEDA, the Trade Act, and Stevenson-Wydler (States and political subdivisions of States and non-profit organizations, as applicable) are subject to the Uniform Relocation Assistance and Real Property Acquisition Policies Act of 1970, as amended (Pub. L. 91-646; 42 U.S.C. 4601 *et seq.*). *See* 15 CFR part 11 and 49 CFR part 24 for specific compliance requirements.
