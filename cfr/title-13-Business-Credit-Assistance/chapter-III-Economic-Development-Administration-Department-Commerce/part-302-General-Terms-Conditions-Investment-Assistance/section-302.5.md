##### ยง 302.5 Relocation assistance and land acquisition policies. #####

Link to an amendment published at 82 FR 57053, Dec. 1, 2017.

Recipients of EDA Investment Assistance under PWEDA and the Trade Act (States and political subdivisions of States and non-profits organizations, as applicable) are subject to the Uniform Relocation Assistance and Real Property Acquisition Policies Act of 1970, as amended (Pub. L. 91-646; 42 U.S.C. 4601 *et seq.*). *See* 15 CFR part 11 and 49 CFR part 24 for specific compliance requirements.
