##### ยง 120.1025 Off-site reviews and monitoring. #####

Link to an amendment published at 82 FR 39504, Aug. 21, 2017.

SBA may conduct off-site reviews and monitoring of SBA Lenders, Intermediaries, and NTAPs, including SBA Lenders', Intermediaries' or NTAPs' self-assessments.
