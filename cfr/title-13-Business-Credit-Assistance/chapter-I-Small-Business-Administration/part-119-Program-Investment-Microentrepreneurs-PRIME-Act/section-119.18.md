##### ยง 119.18 What are the restrictions against lobbying? #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 62951, Oct. 6, 2020.

No assistance made available under the PRIME program may be expended by a grantee or subgrantee to pay any person to influence, or attempt to influence, any agency, elected official, officer, or employee of a Federal, State, or local government in connection with its participation in the program.
