##### ยง 125.15 May an SDVO SBC have affiliates? #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 70051, Nov. 4, 2020.

A concern may have affiliates provided that the aggregate size of the concern and all its affiliates is small as defined in part 121 of this chapter.
