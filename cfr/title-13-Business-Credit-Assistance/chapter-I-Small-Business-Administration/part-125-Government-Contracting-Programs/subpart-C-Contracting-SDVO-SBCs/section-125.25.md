##### ยง 125.25 May SBA appeal a contracting officer's decision not to make a procurement available for award as an SDVO contract? #####

The Administrator may appeal a contracting officer's decision not to make a particular requirement available for award as an SDVO sole source or a SDVO set-aside contact at or above the simplified acquisition threshold.
