##### ยง 129.300 What definitions are important in this subpart? #####

*Covered period* means the period beginning on August 13, 2018 and ending on the date which the Oversight Board established under section 101 of the Puerto Rico Oversight, Management, and Economic Stability Act (48 U.S.C. 2121) terminates. 15 U.S.C. 636(j)(13)(F)(iii).

*Located in Puerto Rico* means a concern with a physical location in Puerto Rico and organized under the laws of Puerto Rico.
