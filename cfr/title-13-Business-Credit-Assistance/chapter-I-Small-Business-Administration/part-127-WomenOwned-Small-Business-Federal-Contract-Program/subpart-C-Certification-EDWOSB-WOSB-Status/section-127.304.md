##### ยง 127.304 How does a concern obtain certification from an approved certifier? #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 27661, May 11, 2020.

A concern that seeks EDWOSB or WOSB certification from an SBA-approved certifier must submit its application directly to the approved certifier in accordance with the specific application procedures of the particular certifier. Any interested party may obtain such certification information and application by contacting the approved certifier at the address provided on SBA's list of approved certifiers.
