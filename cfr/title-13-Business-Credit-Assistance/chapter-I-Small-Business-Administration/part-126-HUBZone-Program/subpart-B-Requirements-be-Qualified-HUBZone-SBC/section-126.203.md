##### ยง 126.203 What size standards apply to HUBZone SBCs? #####

(a) *At time of application for certification.* A HUBZone SBC must meet SBA's size standards for its primary industry classification as defined in ยง 121.201 of this title. If SBA is unable to verify that a concern is small, SBA may deny the concern status as a qualified HUBZone SBC, or SBA may request a formal size determination from the responsible Government Contracting Area Director or designee.

(b) *At time of initial contract offer.* A HUBZone SBC must be small for the size standard corresponding to the NAICS code assigned to the contract.
