##### ยง 126.207 May a qualified HUBZone SBC have offices or facilities in another HUBZone or outside a HUBZone? #####

A qualified HUBZone SBC may have offices or facilities in another HUBZone or even outside a HUBZone and still be a qualified HUBZone SBC. However, in order to be certified as a qualified HUBZone SBC and if required by ยง 126.200, the concern's principal office must be located in a HUBZone.
