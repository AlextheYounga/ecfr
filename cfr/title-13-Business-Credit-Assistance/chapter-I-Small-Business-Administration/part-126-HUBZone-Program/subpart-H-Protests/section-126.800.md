(a) *For sole source procurements.* SBA or the contracting officer may protest the proposed awardee's status as a certified HUBZone small business concern.

(b) *For all other procurements, including Multiple Award Contracts (see ยง 125.1 of this chapter).* SBA, the contracting officer, or any other interested party may protest the apparent successful offeror's status as a certified HUBZone small business concern.
