##### § 40.352 Cigarette tubes. #####

Cigarette tubes are taxed at the following rates under 26 U.S.C. 5701(d):

|                                Product                                 |                         Tax rate for each 50 tubes\* for removals during the years:                          |        |
|------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------|
|                      2002 to  <br/>March 31, 2009                      |                                           April 1, 2009 and after                                            |        |
|                    Cigarette tubes up to 61/2” long                    |                                                   $ 0.0244                                                   |$ 0.0630|
|                    Cigarette tubes over 61/2” long                     |Use rates above, but count each 23/4 inches, or fraction thereof, of the length of each as one cigarette tube.|        |
|\* Tax rate for less than 50 tubes is the same. The tax is not prorated.|                                                                                                              |        |

(72 Stat. 1414; 26 U.S.C. 5701)[T.D. TTB-75, 74 FR 14483, Mar. 31, 2009]
