##### ยง 44.101 Change in individual name. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 87948, Nov. 6, 2024.

Where there is a change in the name of an individual operating as an export warehouse proprietor he shall, within 30 days of such change, make application on Form 2098 (5200.16) for an amended permit.
