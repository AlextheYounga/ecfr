##### ยง 19.353 Bottling tank gauge. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 18726, Apr. 2, 2020.

When a distilled spirits product is to be bottled or packaged, the proprietor must gauge the product after any filtering, reduction, or other treatment, and before bottling or packaging begins. The gauge must be made at labeling or package marking proof, and the details of the gauge must be entered on the bottling and packaging record required in ยง 19.599.
