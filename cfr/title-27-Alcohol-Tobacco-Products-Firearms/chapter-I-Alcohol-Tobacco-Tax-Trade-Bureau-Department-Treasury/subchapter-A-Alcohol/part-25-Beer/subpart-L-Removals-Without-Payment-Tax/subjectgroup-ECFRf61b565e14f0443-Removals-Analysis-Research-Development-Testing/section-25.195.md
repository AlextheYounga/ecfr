##### ยง 25.195 Removals for analysis. #####

A brewer may remove beer, without payment of tax, to a laboratory for analysis to determine the character or quality of the product. Beer may be removed for analysis in packages or in bulk containers. The brewer shall record beer removed for analysis in daily records under ยง 25.292 and on the Brewer's Report of Operations, Form 5130.9.
