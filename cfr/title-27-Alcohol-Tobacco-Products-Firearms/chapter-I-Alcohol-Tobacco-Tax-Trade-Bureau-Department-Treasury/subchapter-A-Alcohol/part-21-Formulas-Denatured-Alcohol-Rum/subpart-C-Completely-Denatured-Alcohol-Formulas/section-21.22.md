##### ยง 21.22 Formula No. 18. #####

To every 100 gallons of ethyl alcohol of not less than 160 proof add:

2.50 gallons of either methyl isobutyl ketone, mixed isomers of nitropropane, or methyl *n-* butyl ketone;0.125 gallon of pyronate or a similar compound;0.50 gallon acetaldol (beta-hydroxybutyraldehyde); and1.00 gallon of either kerosene, deodorized kerosene, gasoline, unleaded gasoline, rubber hydrocarbon solvent, or heptane.
