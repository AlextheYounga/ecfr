##### ยง 18.38 Permanent discontinuance. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 87937, Nov. 6, 2024.

A proprietor who permanently discontinues the business of a concentrate manufacturer shall, after completion of operations, file an application on Form 27-G (5520.3) to cover such discontinuance, giving the date of the discontinuance.
