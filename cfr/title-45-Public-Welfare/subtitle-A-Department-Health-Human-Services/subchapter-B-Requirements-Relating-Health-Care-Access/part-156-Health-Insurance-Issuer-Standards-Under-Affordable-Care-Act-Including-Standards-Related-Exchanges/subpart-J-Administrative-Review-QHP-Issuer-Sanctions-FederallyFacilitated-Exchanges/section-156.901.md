##### ยง 156.901 Definitions. #####

In this subpart, unless the context indicates otherwise:

*ALJ* means administrative law judge of the Departmental Appeals Board of HHS.

*Filing date* means the date postmarked by the U.S. Postal Service, deposited with a carrier for commercial delivery, or hand delivered.

*Hearing* includes a hearing on a written record as well as an in-person or telephone hearing.

*Party* means HHS or the respondent.

*Receipt date* means five days after the date of a document, unless there is a showing that it was in fact received later.

*Respondent* means an entity that received a notice of proposed assessment of a civil money penalty issued pursuant to ยง 156.805 or a notice of decertification pursuant to ยง 156.810(c) or (d).
