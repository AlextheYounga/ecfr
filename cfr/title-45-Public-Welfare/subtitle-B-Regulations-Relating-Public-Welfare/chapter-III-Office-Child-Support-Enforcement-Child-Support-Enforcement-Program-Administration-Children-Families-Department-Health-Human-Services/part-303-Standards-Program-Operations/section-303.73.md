##### ยง 303.73 Applications to use the courts of the United States to enforce court orders. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 35207, June 9, 2020.

The IV-D agency may apply to the Secretary for permission to use a United States district court to enforce a support order of a court of competent jurisdiction against a noncustodial parent who is present in another State if the IV-D agency can furnish evidence in accordance with instructions issued by the office.
