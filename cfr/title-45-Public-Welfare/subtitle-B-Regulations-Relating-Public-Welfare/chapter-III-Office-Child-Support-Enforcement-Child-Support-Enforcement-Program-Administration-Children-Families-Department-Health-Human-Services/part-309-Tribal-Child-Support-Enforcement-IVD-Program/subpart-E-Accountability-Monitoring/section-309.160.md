##### ยง 309.160 How will OCSE determine if Tribal IV-D program funds are appropriately expended? #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93569, Dec. 20, 2016.

OCSE will rely on audits required by 45 CFR part 75. The Department has determined that this program is to be audited as a major program in accordance with section 215(c) of the circular. The Department may supplement the required audits through reviews or audits conducted by its own staff.
