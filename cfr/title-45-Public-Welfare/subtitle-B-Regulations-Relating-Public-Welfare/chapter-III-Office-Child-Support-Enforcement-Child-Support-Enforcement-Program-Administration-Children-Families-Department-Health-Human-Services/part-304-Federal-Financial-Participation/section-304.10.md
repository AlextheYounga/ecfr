##### ยง 304.10 General administrative requirements. #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93566, Dec. 20, 2016.

As a condition for Federal financial participation, the provisions of part 75 of this title (with the exception of 45 CFR 75.306 and 75.341) establishing uniform administrative requirements and cost principles shall apply to all grants made to States under this part.
