##### ยง 2551.45 Is a Senior Companion a federal employee, an employee of the sponsor or of the volunteer station? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 70542, Aug. 30, 2024.

Senior Companions are volunteers, and are not employees of the sponsor, the volunteer station, CNCS, or the Federal Government.
