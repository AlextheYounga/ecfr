##### ยง 2522.440 What weight does AmeriCorps give to each category of the basic criteria? #####

In evaluating applications, AmeriCorps assigns the following weights for each category:

|               Category               |Percentage|
|--------------------------------------|----------|
|            Program design            |    50    |
|      Organizational capability       |    25    |
|Cost-effectiveness and budget adequacy|    25    |
