##### § 2520.60 What government-wide requirements apply to staff fundraising under my AmeriCorps grant? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 46033, May 28, 2024.Link to a correction published at 89 FR 66614, Aug. 16, 2024.

You must follow OMB Guidance published at 2 CFR part 200 and Corporation implementing regulations at 2 CFR Chapter XXII. In particular, see 2 CFR 200.442—Fundraising and Investment Management Costs.
