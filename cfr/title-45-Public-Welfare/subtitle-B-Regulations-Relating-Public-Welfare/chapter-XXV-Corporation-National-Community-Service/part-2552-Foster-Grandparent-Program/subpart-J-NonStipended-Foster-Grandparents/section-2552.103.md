##### ยง 2552.103 Must a sponsor be required to enroll non-stipended Foster Grandparents? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 70544, Aug. 30, 2024.

No. Enrollment of non-stipended Foster Grandparents is not a condition for a sponsor to receive a new or continuation grant.
