##### ยง 2552.45 Is a Foster Grandparent a federal employee, an employee of the sponsor or of the volunteer station? #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 64651, Dec. 17, 2018.

Foster Grandparents are volunteers, and are not employees of the sponsor, the volunteer station, the Corporation, or the Federal Government.
