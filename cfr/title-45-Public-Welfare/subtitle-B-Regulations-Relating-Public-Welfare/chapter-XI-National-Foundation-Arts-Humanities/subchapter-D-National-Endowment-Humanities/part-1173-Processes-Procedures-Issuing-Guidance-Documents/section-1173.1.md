##### ยง 1173.1 Purpose and scope. #####

(a) This part prescribes general procedures that apply to guidance documents issued by the National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH).

(b) This part governs all NEH employees and contractors (collectively, NEH staff) involved with all phases of issuing NEH guidance documents.

(c) This part applies to all NEH guidance documents in effect on or after October 14, 2020.
