##### ยง 1351.15 What costs are supportable under a Runaway and Homeless Youth Program grant? #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93060, December 20, 2016.

Costs which can be supported include, but are not limited to, temporary shelter, referral services, counseling services, aftercare services, and staff training. Costs for acquisition and renovation of existing structures may not normally exceed 15 percent of the grant award. HHS may waive this limitation upon written request under special circumstances based on demonstrated need.
