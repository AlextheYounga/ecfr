##### ยง 1351.11 Who is eligible to apply for a Runaway and Homeless Youth Program grant? #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93060, December 20, 2016.

States localities, private entities, and coordinated networks of such entities are eligible to apply for a Runaway and Homeless Youth Program grant unless they are part of the law enforcement structure or the juvenile justice system.
