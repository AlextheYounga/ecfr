##### ยง 1351.13 What are the Federal and non-Federal match requirements under a Runaway and Homeless Youth grant? #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93060, December 20, 2016.

HHS requires a non-Federal share which is equal to at least 10 percent of the Federal funds that will be received under this grant program for any fiscal year.
