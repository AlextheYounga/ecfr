##### § 301-11.29 Are lodging facilities required to accept a generic federal, state or local tax exempt certificate? #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 39849, July 2, 2020.

Exemptions from taxes for Federal travelers, and the forms required to claim them, vary from location to location. The GSA SmartPay ® Program Support office provides more information regarding state tax exemptions on its Web site (*https://smartpay.gsa.gov/about-gsa-smartpay/tax-information/state-response-letter*) and by e-mail (*gsa\_smartpay@gsa.gov*).
