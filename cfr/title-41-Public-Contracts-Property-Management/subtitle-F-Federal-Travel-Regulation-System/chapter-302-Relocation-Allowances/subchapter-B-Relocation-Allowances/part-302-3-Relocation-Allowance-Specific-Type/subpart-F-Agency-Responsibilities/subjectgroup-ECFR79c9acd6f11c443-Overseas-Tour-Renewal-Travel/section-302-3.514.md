##### ยง 302-3.514 Under what conditions must we pay for tour renewal agreement travel? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 89504, Nov. 13, 2024.

You must pay tour renewal agreement travel when:

(a) The employee has completed the agreed upon period of service outside CONUS;

(b) The employee has agreed to serve another OCONUS tour of duty at the same or different duty station; and

(c) You have determined that the employee meets the special rules under ยง 302-3.515 for Alaska or Hawaii.
