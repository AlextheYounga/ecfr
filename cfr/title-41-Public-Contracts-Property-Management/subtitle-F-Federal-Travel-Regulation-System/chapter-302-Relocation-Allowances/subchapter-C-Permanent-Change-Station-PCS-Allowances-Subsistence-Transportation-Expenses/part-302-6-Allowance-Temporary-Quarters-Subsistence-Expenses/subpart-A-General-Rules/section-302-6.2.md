##### § 302-6.2 What are “temporary quarters subsistence expenses (TQSE)”? #####

“Temporary quarters subsistence expenses” or “TQSE” are subsistence expenses incurred by an employee and/or his/her immediate family while occupying temporary quarters. TQSE does not include transportation expenses incurred during occupancy of temporary quarters (see § 302-6.18 for details).
