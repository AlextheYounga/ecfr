##### ยง 102-118.490 What if my agency fails to settle a dispute within 30 days? #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 93197, Nov. 26, 2024.

(a) If your agency fails to settle a dispute within 30 days, the TSP may appeal to: General Services Administration, Office of Travel, Employee Relocation and Transportation, Attn: Transportation Audits Division, 1800 F Street NW, 3rd Floor, Mail Hub 3400, Washington, DC 20405; or electronically at: *https://tams.gsa.gov.*

(b) If the TSP disagrees with the administrative settlement by the GSA Transportation Audits Division, the TSP may appeal to the Civilian Board of Contract Appeals.
