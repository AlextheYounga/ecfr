##### § 102-118.105 Where can I find the rules governing the use of a Government Bill of Lading? #####

The “U.S. Government Freight Transportation - Handbook” contains information on how to prepare this GBL form. To get a copy of this handbook, you may write to: General Services Administration, Transportation Audit Division (QMCA), Crystal Plaza 4, Room 300, 2200 Crystal Drive, Arlington, VA 22202, *www.gsa.gov/transaudits.*
