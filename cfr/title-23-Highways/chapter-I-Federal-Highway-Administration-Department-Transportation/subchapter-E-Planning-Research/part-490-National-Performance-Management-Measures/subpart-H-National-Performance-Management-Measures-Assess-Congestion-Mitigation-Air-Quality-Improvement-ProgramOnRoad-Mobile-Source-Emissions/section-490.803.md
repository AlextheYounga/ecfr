##### ยง 490.803 Applicability. #####

(a) The on-road mobile source emissions performance measure (called the Total Emissions Reduction- see ยง 490.807) is applicable to all States and MPOs with projects financed with funds from the 23 U.S.C. 149 CMAQ program apportioned to State DOTs for areas designated as nonattainment or maintenance for ozone (O3), carbon monoxide (CO), or particulate matter (PM10 and PM2.5) National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
