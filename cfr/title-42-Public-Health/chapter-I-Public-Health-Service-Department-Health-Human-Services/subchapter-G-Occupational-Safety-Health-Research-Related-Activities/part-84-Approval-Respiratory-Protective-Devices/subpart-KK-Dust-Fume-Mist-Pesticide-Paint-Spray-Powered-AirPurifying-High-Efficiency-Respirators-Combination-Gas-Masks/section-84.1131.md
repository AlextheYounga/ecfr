##### ยง 84.1131 Respirators; required components. #####

(a) Each respirator described in ยง 84.1130 shall, where its design requires, contain the following component parts:

(1) Facepiece, mouthpiece with noseclip, hood, or helmet;

(2) Filter unit, canister with filter, or cartridge with filter;

(4) Attached blower; and
