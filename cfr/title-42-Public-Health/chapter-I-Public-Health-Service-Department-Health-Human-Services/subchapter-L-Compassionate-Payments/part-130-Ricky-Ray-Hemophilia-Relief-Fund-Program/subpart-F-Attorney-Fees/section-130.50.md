##### ยง 130.50 Limitation on agent and attorney fees. #####

As provided by section 107 of the Act:

(a) Notwithstanding any contract, the representative of an individual may not receive, for services rendered in connection with the petition of an individual under this Act, more than 5 percent of a payment made under this Act (*i.e.,* $5,000) on the petition.

(b) Any such representative who violates this section is subject to a fine of not more than $50,000.
