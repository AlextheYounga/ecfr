##### ยง 510.620 Waiver of deductible and coinsurance that otherwise apply to reconciliation payments or repayments. #####

Link to an amendment published at 82 FR 622, Jan. 3, 2017.This amendment was delayed until Mar. 21, 2017 at 82 FR 10863, Feb. 17, 2017.This amendment was further delayed to May 20, 2017 at 82 FR 14464, Mar. 21, 2017.

(a) *Waiver of deductible and coinsurance.* CMS waives the requirements of sections 1813 and 1822(a) of the Act for Medicare Part A and Part B payment systems only to the extent necessary to make reconciliation payments or receive repayments based on the NPRA that reflect the episode payment methodology under the final payment model for CJR participant hospitals.

(b) *Reconciliation payments or repayments.* Reconciliation payments or repayments do not affect the beneficiary cost-sharing amounts for the Part A and Part B services provided under the CJR model.
