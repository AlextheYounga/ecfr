##### ยง 512.900 Termination of an episode payment model. #####

CMS may terminate any EPM for reasons including but not limited to:

(a) CMS no longer has the funds to support the EPM; or

(b) CMS terminates the EPM in accordance with section 1115A(b)(3)(B) of the Act. As provided by section 1115A(d)(2) of the Act, termination of the model is not subject to administrative or judicial review.
