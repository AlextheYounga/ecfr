##### ยง 512.905 Termination of the CR incentive payment model. #####

CMS may terminate the CR incentive payment model for reasons including, but not limited to, one of the following:

(a) CMS no longer has the funds to support the CR incentive payment model.

(b) CMS terminates the CR incentive payment model in accordance with section 1115A(b)(3)(B) of the Act. As provided by section 1115A(d)(2) of the Act, termination of the model is not subject to administrative or judicial review.
