##### ยง 512.735 Enforcement authority for FFS-CR participants. #####

(a) *OIG authority.* OIG authority is not limited or restricted by the provisions of the CR incentive payment model, including the authority to audit, evaluate, investigate, or inspect the FFS-CR participant, or any other person or entity or their records, data, or information, without limitation.

(b) *Other authorities.* None of the provisions of the CR incentive payment model limits or restricts the authority of any other government agency permitted by law to audit, evaluate, investigate, or inspect the FFS-CR participant or any other person or entity or their records, data, or information, without limitation.
