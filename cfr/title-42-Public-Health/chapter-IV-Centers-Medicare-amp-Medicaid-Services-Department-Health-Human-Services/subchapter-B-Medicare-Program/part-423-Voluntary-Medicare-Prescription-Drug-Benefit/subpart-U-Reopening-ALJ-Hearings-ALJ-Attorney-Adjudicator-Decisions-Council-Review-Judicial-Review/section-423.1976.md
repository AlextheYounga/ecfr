##### ยง 423.1976 Judicial review. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 19872, May 7, 2019.

(a) *Review of ALJ's or attorney adjudicator's decision.* The enrollee may request judicial review of an ALJ's or attorney adjudicator's decision if -

(1) The Council denied the enrollee's request for review; and

(2) The amount in controversy meets the threshold requirement established annually by the Secretary.

(b) *Review of Council decision.* The enrollee may request judicial review of the Council decision if it is the final decision of CMS and the amount in controversy meets the threshold established in paragraph (a)(2) of this section.

(c) *How to request judicial review.* In order to request judicial review, an enrollee must file a civil action in a district court of the United States in accordance with section 205(g) of the Act. (See ยง 423.2136 for a description of the procedures to follow in requesting judicial review.)
