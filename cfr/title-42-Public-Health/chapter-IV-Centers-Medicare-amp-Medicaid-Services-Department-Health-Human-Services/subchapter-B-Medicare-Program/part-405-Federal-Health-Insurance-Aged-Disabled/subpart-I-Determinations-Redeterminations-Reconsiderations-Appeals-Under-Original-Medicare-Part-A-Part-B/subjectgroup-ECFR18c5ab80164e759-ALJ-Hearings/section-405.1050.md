##### ยง 405.1050 Removal of a hearing request from an ALJ to the MAC. #####

Link to an amendment published at 82 FR 5119, Jan. 17, 2017.

If a request for hearing is pending before an ALJ, the MAC may assume responsibility for holding a hearing by requesting that the ALJ send the hearing request to it. If the MAC holds a hearing, it conducts the hearing according to the rules for hearings before an ALJ. Notice is mailed to all parties at their last known address informing them that the MAC has assumed responsibility for the case.
