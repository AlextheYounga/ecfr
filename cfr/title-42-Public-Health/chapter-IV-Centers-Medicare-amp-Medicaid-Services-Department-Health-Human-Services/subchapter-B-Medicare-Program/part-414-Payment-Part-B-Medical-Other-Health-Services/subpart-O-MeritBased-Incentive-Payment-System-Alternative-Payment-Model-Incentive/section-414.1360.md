##### ยง 414.1360 Data submission criteria for the improvement activities performance category. #####

(a) For purposes of the transition year of MIPS and future years, MIPS eligible clinicians or groups must submit data on MIPS improvement activities in one of the following manners:

(1) *Via direct, login and upload, and login and attest.* For the applicable performance period, submit a yes response for each improvement activity that is performed for at least a continuous 90-day period during the applicable performance period.

(i) Submit a yes response for activities within the improvement activities inventory.
