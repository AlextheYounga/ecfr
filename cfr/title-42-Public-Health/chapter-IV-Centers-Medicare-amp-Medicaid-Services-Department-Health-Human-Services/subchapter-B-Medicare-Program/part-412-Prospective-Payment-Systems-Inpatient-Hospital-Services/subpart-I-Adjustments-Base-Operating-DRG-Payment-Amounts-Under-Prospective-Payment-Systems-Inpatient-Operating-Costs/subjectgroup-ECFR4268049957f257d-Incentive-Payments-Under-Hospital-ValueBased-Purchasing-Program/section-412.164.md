##### ยง 412.164 Measure selection under the Hospital Value-Based Purchasing (VBP) Program. #####

(a) CMS will select measures, other than measures of readmissions, for purposes of the Hospital VBP Program. The measures will be a subset of the measures specified under section 1886(b)(3)(B)(viii) of the Act (the Hospital Inpatient Quality Reporting Program).

(b) CMS will post data on each measure on the *Hospital Compare* Web site for at least 1 year prior to the beginning of a performance period for the measure under the Hospital VBP Program.
