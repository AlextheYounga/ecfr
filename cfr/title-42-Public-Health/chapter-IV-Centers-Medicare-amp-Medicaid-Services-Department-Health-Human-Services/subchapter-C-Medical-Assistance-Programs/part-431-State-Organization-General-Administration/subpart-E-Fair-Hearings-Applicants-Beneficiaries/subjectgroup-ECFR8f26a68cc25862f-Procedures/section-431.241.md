##### ยง 431.241 Matters to be considered at the hearing. #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 86449, Nov. 30, 2016.

The hearing must cover -

(a) Agency action or failure to act with reasonable promptness on a claim for services, including both initial and subsequent decisions regarding eligibility;

(b) Agency decisions regarding changes in the type or amount of services;

(c) A decision by a skilled nursing facility or nursing facility to transfer or discharge a resident; and

(d) A State determination with regard to the preadmission screening and annual resident review requirements of section 1919(e)(7) of the Act.
