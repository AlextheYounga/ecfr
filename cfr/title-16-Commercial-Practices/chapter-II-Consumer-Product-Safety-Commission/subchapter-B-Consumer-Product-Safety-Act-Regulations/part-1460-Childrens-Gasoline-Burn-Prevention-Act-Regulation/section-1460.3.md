##### ยง 1460.3 Requirements for child-resistance for closures on portable gasoline containers. #####

Each portable gasoline container manufactured on or after April 12, 2015 for sale in the United States shall conform to the child-resistance requirements for closures on portable gasoline containers specified in sections 2 through 6 of ASTM F2517-15 (including Appendixes X2 and X3 referenced therein), *Standard Specification for Determination of Child Resistance of Portable Fuel Containers for Consumer Use,* approved on January 1, 2015. The Director of the Federal Register approves the incorporation by reference listed in this section in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51.
