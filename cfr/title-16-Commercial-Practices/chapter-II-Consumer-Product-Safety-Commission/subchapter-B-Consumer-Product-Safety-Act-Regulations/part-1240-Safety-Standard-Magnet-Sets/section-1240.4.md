##### ยง 1240.4 Test procedure for determining flux index. #####

(a) Select at least one magnet of each shape and size in the magnet set.

(b) Measure the flux index of each selected magnet in accordance with the procedure in sections 8.24.1 through 8.24.3 of ASTM F963-11, *Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Toy Safety,* approved on December 1, 2011.
