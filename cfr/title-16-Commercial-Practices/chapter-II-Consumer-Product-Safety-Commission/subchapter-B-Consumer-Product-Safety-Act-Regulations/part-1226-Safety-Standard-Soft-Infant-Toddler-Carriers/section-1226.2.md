##### ยง 1226.2 Requirements for soft infant and toddler carriers. #####

Each soft infant and toddler carrier must comply with all applicable provisions of ASTM F2236-24, *Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Soft Infant and Toddler Carriers,* approved on July 1, 2024. The Director of the Federal Register approves this incorporation by reference in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51. This material is available for inspection at the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission and at the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA).
