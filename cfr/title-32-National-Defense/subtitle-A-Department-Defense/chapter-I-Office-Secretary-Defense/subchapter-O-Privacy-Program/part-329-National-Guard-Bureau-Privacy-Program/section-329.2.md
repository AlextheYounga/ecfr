##### § 329.2 Applicability. #####

(a) This part applies to the NGB and the records under control of the Chief, NGB, as defined by DoD Directive (DoDD) 5105.77, entitled “National Guard Bureau.” (Available at *http://*

*www.dtic.mil/whs/directives/corres/pdf**/510577p.pdf*)

(b) This rule will cover the privacy policies and procedures associated with records created and under the control of the Chief, NGB that are not otherwise covered by existing DoD, Air Force, or Army rules.
