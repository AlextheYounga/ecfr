##### § 54.1 Purpose. #####

Under section 65 of title 42, United States Code, this part provides policy on statutorily required child or child and spousal support allotments, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures. The policy and procedures for this part are also located in the DoD Financial Management Regulation (“DoDFMR”), Volume 7B, Chapter 43, section 4304, “Allotments for Child Support and Spousal Support” (DoD 7000.14-R).
