##### § 21.220 What publications are in the DGARS? #####

A DoD publication (DoD 3210.6-R2) entitled “The DoD Grant and Agreement Regulations” is the principal element of the DGARS. The Director of Defense Research and Engineering also may publish DGARS policies and procedures in DoD instructions and other DoD publications, as appropriate.
