##### ยง 1700.4 Preliminary information. #####

Members of the public shall address all communications to the point of contact specified in ยง 1700.3 and clearly delineate the communication as a request under the FOIA. ODNI staff who receive a FOIA request shall expeditiously forward the request to the Director, Information Management Office (IMO).
