##### ยง 1003.11 Address for filing documents. #####

All applications, requests, petitions, appeals, written communications and other documents to be submitted to or filed with the OHA, as provided in this part or otherwise, shall be addressed to the Office of Hearings and Appeals, U.S. Department of Energy, 1000 Independence Avenue, SW., Washington, DC 20585-0107. The OHA has facilities for the receipt of transmissions via FAX, at FAX Number (202) 426-1415.
