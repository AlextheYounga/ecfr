##### ยง 474.5 Review and Update. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 22059, Mar. 29, 2024.

The Department will review part 474 five years after the date of publication as a final rule to determine whether any updates and/or revisions are necessary. DOE will publish a notice in the Federal Register soliciting stakeholder input in this review. The Department will publish the findings of the review and any resulting adjustments to part 474 in the Federal Register.
