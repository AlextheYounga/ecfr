##### ยง 95.3 Byproducts from diseased animals prohibited. #####

The importation of any animal byproduct taken or removed from an animal affected with anthrax, foot-and-mouth disease, highly pathogenic avian influenza, Newcastle disease, or rinderpest is prohibited.
