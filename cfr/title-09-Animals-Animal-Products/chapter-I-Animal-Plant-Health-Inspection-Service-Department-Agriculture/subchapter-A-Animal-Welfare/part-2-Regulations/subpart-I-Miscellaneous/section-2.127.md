##### ยง 2.127 Publication of names of persons subject to the provisions of this part. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 28798, May 13, 2020.

APHIS will publish lists of persons licensed or registered in accordance with the provisions of this part in the Federal Register. The lists may be obtained upon request from the AC Regional Director.
