##### ยง 3.10 Watering. #####

If potable water is not continually available to the dogs and cats, it must be offered to the dogs and cats as often as necessary to ensure their health and well-being, but not less than twice daily for at least 1 hour each time, unless restricted by the attending veterinarian. Water receptacles must be kept clean and sanitized in accordance with ยง 3.11(b) of this subpart, and before being used to water a different dog or cat or social grouping of dogs or cats.
