##### § 29.1393 Minimum intensities in any vertical plane of forward and rear position lights. #####

Each position light intensity must equal or exceed the applicable values in the following table:

|Angle above or below the horizontal plane|Intensity, I|
|-----------------------------------------|------------|
|                   0°                    |    1.00    |
|                0° to 5°                 |    .90     |
|                5° to 10°                |    .80     |
|               10° to 15°                |    .70     |
|               15° to 20°                |    .50     |
|               20° to 30°                |    .30     |
|               30° to 40°                |    .10     |
|               40° to 90°                |    .05     |
