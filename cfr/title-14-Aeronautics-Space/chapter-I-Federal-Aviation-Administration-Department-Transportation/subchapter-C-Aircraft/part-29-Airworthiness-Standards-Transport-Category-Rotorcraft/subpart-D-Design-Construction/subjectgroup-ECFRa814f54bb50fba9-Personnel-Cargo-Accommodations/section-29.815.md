##### ยง 29.815 Main aisle width. #####

The main passenger aisle width between seats must equal or exceed the values in the following table:

|                                                      Passenger seating capacity                                                      |  Minimum main passenger aisle width  |   |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|---|
|                                               Less than 25 inches from floor (inches)                                                |25 Inches and more from floor (inches)|   |
|                                                              10 or less                                                              |                  12                  |15 |
|                                                            11 through 19                                                             |                  12                  |20 |
|                                                              20 or more                                                              |                  15                  |20 |
|<sup>1</sup> A narrower width not less than 9 inches may be approved when substantiated by tests found necessary by the Administrator.|                                      |   |
