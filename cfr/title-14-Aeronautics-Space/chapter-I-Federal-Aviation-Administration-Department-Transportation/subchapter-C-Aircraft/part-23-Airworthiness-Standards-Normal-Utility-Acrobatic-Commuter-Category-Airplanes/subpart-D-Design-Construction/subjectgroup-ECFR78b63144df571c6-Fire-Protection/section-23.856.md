##### § 23.856 Thermal/acoustic insulation materials. #####

Thermal/acoustic insulation material installed in the fuselage must meet the flame propagation test requirements of part II of Appendix F to this part, or other approved equivalent test requirements. This requirement does not apply to “small parts,” as defined in § 23.853(d)(3)(v).
