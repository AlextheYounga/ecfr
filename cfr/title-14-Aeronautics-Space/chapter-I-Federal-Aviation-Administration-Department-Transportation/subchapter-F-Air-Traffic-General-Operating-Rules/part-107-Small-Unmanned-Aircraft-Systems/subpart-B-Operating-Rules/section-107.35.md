##### ยง 107.35 Operation of multiple small unmanned aircraft. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 4382, Jan. 15, 2021.This amendment delayed until Apr. 21, 2021 at 86 FR 13630, Mar. 10, 2021.

A person may not operate or act as a remote pilot in command or visual observer in the operation of more than one unmanned aircraft at the same time.
