##### ยง 13.301 Inflation adjustments of civil monetary penalties. #####

(a) This subpart provides the maximum civil monetary penalties or range of minimum and maximum civil monetary penalties for each statutory civil penalty subject to FAA jurisdiction, as adjusted for inflation.

(b) Each adjustment to a maximum civil monetary penalty or to minimum and maximum civil monetary penalties that establish a civil monetary penalty range applies to actions initiated under this part for violations occurring on or after January 6, 2023, notwithstanding references to specific civil penalty amounts elsewhere in this part.
