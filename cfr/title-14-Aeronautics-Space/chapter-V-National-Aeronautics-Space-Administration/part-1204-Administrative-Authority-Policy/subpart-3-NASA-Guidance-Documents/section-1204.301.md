##### ยง 1204.301 Review and clearance. #####

All NASA guidance documents, as defined in ยง 1204.300(c), require review and clearance in accordance with this subpart.

(a) Guidance proposed by a NASA responsible office must be reviewed by the head of the relevant legal practice group within NASA's Office of General Counsel (OGC) and cleared by the General Counsel.

(b) Additional reviews by other NASA offices are also conducted and are described in NPD 1400.2, Publishing NASA Documents in the Federal Register and Responding to Regulatory Actions, *https://nodis3.gsfc.nasa.gov/displayDir.cfm?t=NPD&c=1400&s=2E*.
