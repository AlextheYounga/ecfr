##### § 2590.736 Applicability dates. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 23416, Apr. 3, 2024.

Sections 2590.701-1 through 2590.701-8 and 2590.731 through 2590.736 are applicable for plan years beginning on or after July 1, 2005. Until the applicability date for this regulation, plans and issuers are required to continue to comply with the corresponding sections of 29 CFR part 2590, contained in the 29 CFR, parts 1927 to end, edition revised as of July 1, 2004. Notwithstanding the previous sentence, the definition of “short-term, limited-duration insurance” in § 2590.701-2 applies October 2, 2018.
