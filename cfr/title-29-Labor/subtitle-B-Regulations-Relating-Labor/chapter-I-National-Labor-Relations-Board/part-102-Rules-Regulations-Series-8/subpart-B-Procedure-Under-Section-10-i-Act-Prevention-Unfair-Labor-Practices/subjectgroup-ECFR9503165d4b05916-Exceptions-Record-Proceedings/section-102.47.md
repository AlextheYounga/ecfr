##### ยง 102.47 Filing of motion after transfer of case to Board. #####

All motions filed after the case has been transferred to the Board pursuant to ยง 102.45 shall be filed with the Board in Washington, DC, by transmitting eight copies thereof, together with an affidavit of service on the parties.
