##### ยง 1473.6 Waiver of publication of guidance documents. #####

(a) Section 1473.5(b) and (e) does not apply to guidance documents for which a waiver has been applied from the OMB Director pursuant to Subsection 3(c) of E.O. 13891.

(b) Requests for waivers must be written and signed by a senior policy official at the agency.
