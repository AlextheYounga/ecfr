##### ยง 405.5 Special reports. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 33842, July 18, 2018.

In addition to the report on Form LM-10, the Office of Labor-Management Standards may require from employers subject to the Act the submission of special reports on pertinent information, including but not necessarily confined to reports with respect to specifically identified personnel on the matters referred to in the instructions for Part A of the Form LM-10.
