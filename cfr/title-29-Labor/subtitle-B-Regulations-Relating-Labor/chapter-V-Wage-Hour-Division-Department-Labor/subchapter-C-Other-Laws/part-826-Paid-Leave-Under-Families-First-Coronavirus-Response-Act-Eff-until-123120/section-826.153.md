##### ยง 826.153 Investigative authority of the Secretary. #####

(a) *Investigative authority under the EPSLA.* For purposes of the EPSLA, the Secretary has the investigative authority and subpoena authority set forth in sections 9 and 11 of the FLSA, 29 U.S.C. 209, 211.

(b) *Investigative authority under the EFMLEA.* For purposes of EFMLEA, the Secretary has the investigative authority set forth in section 106(a) of the FMLA, 29 U.S.C. 2616(a), and the subpoena authority set forth in section 106(d) of the FMLA, 29 U.S.C. 2616(d).
