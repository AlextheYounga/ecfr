##### ยง 530.408 Notice of the Secretary to review decision. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 1787, Jan. 11, 2021.

Whenever the Secretary determines to review the decision and order of an Administrative Law Judge, the Secretary shall notify each party of the issue or issues raised; the form in which submission shall be made (*i.e.,* briefs, oral argument, etc.); and, the time within which such presentation shall be submitted.
