##### ยง 4901.3 Electronic reading room. #####

Link to an amendment published at 87 FR 43994, July 25, 2022.

The PBGC will maintain an electronic reading room on its Web site, *www.pbgc.gov,* where persons may inspect in an electronic format all records made available for such purposes under this part.
