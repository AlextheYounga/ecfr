(1) When an initial determination is issued by a Department Director, the Department Director (or an official designated by the Department Director) will issue the final decision on request for reconsideration of a determination other than one described in ยง 4003.1(b)(3)(ii).

(2) The Director (or an official designated by the Director) will issue the final decision on a request for reconsideration of a determination described in ยง 4003.1(b)(3)(ii).

(b) The final decision on a request for reconsideration shall be in writing, specify the relief granted, if any, state the reason(s) for the decision, and state that the person has exhausted his or her administrative remedies.
