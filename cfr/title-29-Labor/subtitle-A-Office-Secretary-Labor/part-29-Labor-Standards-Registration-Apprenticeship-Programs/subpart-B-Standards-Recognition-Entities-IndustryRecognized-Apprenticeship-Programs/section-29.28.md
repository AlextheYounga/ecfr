##### ยง 29.28 Derecognition's effect on Industry-Recognized Apprenticeship Programs. #####

(a) Following its SRE's derecognition, an IRAP will maintain its status until 1 year after the Administrator's decision derecognizing the IRAP's SRE becomes final, including any appeals. At the end of 1 year, the IRAP will lose its status unless it is already recognized by another SRE recognized under this subpart.

(b) Upon derecognizing an SRE, the Administrator will update the public list described in ยง 29.24 to reflect the derecognition, and the Administrator will notify the SRE's IRAP(s) of the derecognition.
