##### ยง 502.150 Further evidence required by presiding officer during hearing. #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93836, Dec. 22, 2016.Link to a correction published at 82 FR 46, Jan. 3, 2017.This amendment and its correction were delayed until Apr. 3, 2017 at 82 FR 8903, Feb. 1, 2017.

At any time during the hearing, the presiding officer may call for further evidence upon any issue, and require such evidence where available to be presented by the party or parties concerned, either at the hearing or adjournment thereof. [Rule 150.]
