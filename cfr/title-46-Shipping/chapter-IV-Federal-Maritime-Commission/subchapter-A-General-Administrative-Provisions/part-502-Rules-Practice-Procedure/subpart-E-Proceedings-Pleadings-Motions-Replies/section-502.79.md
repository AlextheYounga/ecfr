##### ยง 502.79 Consolidation of proceedings. #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 93836, Dec. 22, 2016.Link to a correction published at 82 FR 46, Jan. 3, 2017.This amendment and its correction were delayed until Apr. 3, 2017 at 82 FR 8903, Feb. 1, 2017.

The Commission or the Chief Administrative Law Judge (or designee) may order two or more proceedings which involve substantially the same issues consolidated and heard together.
