##### § 111.60-6 Fiber optic cable. #####

Link to an amendment published at 88 FR 16363, Mar. 16, 2023.

Each fiber optic cable must—

(a) Be constructed to pass the flammability test contained in IEEE 1202, test VW-1 of UL 1581, or Category A of IEC 60332-3-22 (all three standards incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1); or

(b) Be installed in accordance with § 111.60-2.
