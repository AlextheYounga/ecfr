##### ยง 111.33-11 Propulsion systems. #####

Link to an amendment published at 88 FR 16362, Mar. 16, 2023.

Each power semiconductor rectifier system in a propulsion system must meet sections 4-8-5/5.17.9 and 4-8-5/5.17.10 of ABS Steel Vessel Rules (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1), except that each one for mobile offshore drilling units must meet the requirements in Part 4, Chapter 3, section 4/3.5.3 of ABS MODU Rules (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1).
