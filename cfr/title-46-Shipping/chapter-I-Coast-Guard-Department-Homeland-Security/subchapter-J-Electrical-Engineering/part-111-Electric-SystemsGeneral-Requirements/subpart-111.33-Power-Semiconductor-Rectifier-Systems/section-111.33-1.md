##### § 111.33-1 General. #####

Link to an amendment published at 88 FR 16361, Mar. 16, 2023.

This subpart is applicable to all power semiconductor rectifier systems. In addition to the regulations contained in this subpart, the requirements of §§ 111.30-11 and 111.30-19 of this part must be met, if applicable.
