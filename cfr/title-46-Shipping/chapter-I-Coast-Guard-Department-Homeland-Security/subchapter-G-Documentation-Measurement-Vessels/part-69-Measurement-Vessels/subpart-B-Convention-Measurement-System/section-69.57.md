##### ยง 69.57 Gross tonnage ITC. #####

Gross tonnage ITC (GT ITC) is determined by the following formula GT ITC = K1 V, in which V = total volume of all enclosed spaces in cubic meters and K1 = 0.2 + 0.02 log10 V.
