##### ยง 11.603 Requirements for Radio Officer endorsements. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 102336, Dec. 17, 2024.

Each applicant for an original endorsement or renewal of License must present a current Radiotelegraph Operator License (T) issued by the Federal Communications Commission. The applicant must enter on the endorsement application form the number, class, and date of issuance of their Federal Communications Commission License.
