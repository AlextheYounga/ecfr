##### ยง 50.10-23 Marine Safety Center. #####

The term *Marine Safety Center* refers to the Commanding Officer, Marine Safety Center, U.S. Coast Guard, 4200 Wilson Boulevard, Suite 400, Arlington, VA 22203 for visitors. Send all mail to Commanding Officer (MSC), Attn: Marine Safety Center, U.S. Coast Guard Stop 7410, 4200 Wilson Boulevard, Suite 400, Arlington, VA 20598-7410, in a written or electronic format. Information for submitting the VSP electronically can be found at *http://www.uscg.mil/HQ/MSC.*
