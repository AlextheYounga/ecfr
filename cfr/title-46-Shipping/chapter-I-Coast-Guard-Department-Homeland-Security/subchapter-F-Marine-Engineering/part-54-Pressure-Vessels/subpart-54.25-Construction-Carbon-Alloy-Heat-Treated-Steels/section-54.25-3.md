##### ยง 54.25-3 Steel plates (modifies UCS-6). #####

The steels listed in UCS-6(b) of section VIII of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 54.01-1) will be allowed only in Class III pressure vessels (see table 54.01-5(b)).
