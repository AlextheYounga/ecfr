##### ยง 665.267 Seasons. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 26624, May 5, 2020.

The fishing year for precious coral begins on July 1 and ends on June 30 the following year, except at the Makapu'u and Au'au Channel Beds, which have a two-year fishing period that begins July 1 and ends June 30, two years later.
