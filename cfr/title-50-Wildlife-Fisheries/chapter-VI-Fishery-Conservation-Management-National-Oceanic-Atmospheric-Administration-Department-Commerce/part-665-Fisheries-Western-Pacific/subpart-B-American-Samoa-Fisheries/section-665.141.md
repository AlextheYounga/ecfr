##### § 665.141 Definitions. #####

As used in §§ 665.140 through 665.159:

*American Samoa crustacean management unit species* means the following crustaceans:

|Samoan name|English common name|               Scientific name               |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
|    Ula    |   spiny lobster   |Panulirus marginatus, Panulirus penicillatus.|
|  Papata   |  slipper lobster  |                Scyllaridae.                 |
|   pa'a    |     Kona crab     |               Ranina ranina.                |
|           | deepwater shrimp  |              Heterocarpus spp.              |

*Crustacean Permit Area 3 (Permit Area 3)* includes the EEZ around American Samoa.
