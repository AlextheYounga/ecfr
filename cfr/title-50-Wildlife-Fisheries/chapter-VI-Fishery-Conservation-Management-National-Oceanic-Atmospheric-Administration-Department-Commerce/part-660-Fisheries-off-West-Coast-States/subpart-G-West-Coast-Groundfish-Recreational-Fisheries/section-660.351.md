##### ยง 660.351 Recreational fishery - definitions. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 13431, Mar. 29, 2018.

These definitions are specific to the recreational fisheries covered in this subpart. General groundfish definitions are defined at ยง 660.11, subpart C.

*Bag limit* means the number of fish available to an angler.

*Boat limit* means the number of fish available to for a vessel or boat.

*Hook limit* means a limit on the number of hooks on any given fishing line.
