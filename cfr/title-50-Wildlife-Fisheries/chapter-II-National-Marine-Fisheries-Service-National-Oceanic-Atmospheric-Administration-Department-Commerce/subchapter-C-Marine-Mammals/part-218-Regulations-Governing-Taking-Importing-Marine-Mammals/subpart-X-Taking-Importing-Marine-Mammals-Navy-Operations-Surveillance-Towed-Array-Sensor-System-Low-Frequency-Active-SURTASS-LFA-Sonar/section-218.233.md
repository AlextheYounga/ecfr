##### § 218.233 Prohibitions. #####

No person in connection with the activities described in § 218.230 may:

(a) Take any marine mammal not specified in § 218.230(b);

(b) Take any marine mammal specified in § 218.230 other than by incidental take as specified in § 218.232(c)(2) through (5);

(c) Take any marine mammal specified in § 218.230 if NMFS makes a determination that such taking results in more than a negligible impact on the species or stocks of such marine mammal; or

(d) Violate, or fail to comply with, any of the terms, conditions, or requirements of these regulations or a Letter of Authorization issued under §§ 216.106 and 218.238 of this chapter.
