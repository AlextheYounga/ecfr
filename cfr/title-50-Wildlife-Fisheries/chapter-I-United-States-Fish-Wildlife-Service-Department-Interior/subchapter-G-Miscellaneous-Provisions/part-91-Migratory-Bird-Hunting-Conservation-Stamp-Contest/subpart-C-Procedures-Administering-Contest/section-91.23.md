##### § 91.23 Scoring criteria for contest. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 47596, Aug. 26, 2021.

Entries will be judged on the basis of anatomical accuracy, artistic composition, suitability for reduction in the production of a stamp, and how well they illustrate the theme of “celebrating our waterfowl hunting heritage.”
