##### ยง 200.426 Bad debts. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 49565, Aug. 13, 2020.

Bad debts (debts which have been determined to be uncollectable), including losses (whether actual or estimated) arising from uncollectable accounts and other claims, are unallowable. Related collection costs, and related legal costs, arising from such debts after they have been determined to be uncollectable are also unallowable.
