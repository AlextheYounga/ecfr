##### ยง 401.58 Pleasure craft scheduling. #####

Link to an amendment published at 87 FR 12591, Mar. 7, 2022.

(a) The transit of pleasure craft shall be scheduled by the traffic controller or the officer in charge of a lock and may be delayed so as to avoid interference with other vessels; and

(b) Every pleasure craft seeking to transit Canadian locks shall stop at a pleasure craft dock and arrange for transit by contacting the lock personnel using the direct line.
