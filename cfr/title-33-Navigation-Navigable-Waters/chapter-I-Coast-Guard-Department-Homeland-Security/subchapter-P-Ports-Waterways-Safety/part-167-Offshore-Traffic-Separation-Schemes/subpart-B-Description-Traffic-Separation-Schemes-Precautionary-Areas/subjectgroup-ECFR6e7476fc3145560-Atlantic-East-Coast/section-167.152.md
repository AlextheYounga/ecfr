##### § 167.152 Off New York: Eastern approach, off Nantucket. #####

(a) A separation zone is established bounded by a line connecting the following geographical positions:

| Latitude  | Longitude |
|-----------|-----------|
|40°28.75′ N|69°14.83′ W|
|40°27.62′ N|70°13.77′ W|
|40°30.62′ N|70°14.00′ W|
|40°31.75′ N|69°14.97′ W|

(b) A traffic lane for westbound traffic is established between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographical positions:

| Latitude  | Longitude |
|-----------|-----------|
|40°36.75′ N|69°15.17′ W|
|40°35.62′ N|70°14.15′ W|

| Latitude  | Longitude |
|-----------|-----------|
|40°22.62′ N|70°13.60′ W|
|40°23.75′ N|69°14.63′ W|
