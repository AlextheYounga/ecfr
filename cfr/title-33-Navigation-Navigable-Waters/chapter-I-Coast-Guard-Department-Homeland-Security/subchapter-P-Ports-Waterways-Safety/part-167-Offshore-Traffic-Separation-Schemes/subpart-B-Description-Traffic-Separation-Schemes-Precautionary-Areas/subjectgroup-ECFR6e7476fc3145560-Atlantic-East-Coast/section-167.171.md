##### § 167.171 Off Delaware Bay: Eastern approach. #####

(a) A separation zone is established bounded by a line connecting the following geographic positions:

| Latitude  | Longitude |
|-----------|-----------|
|38°46.30′ N|74°34.45′ W|
|38°46.33′ N|74°55.75′ W|
|38°47.45′ N|74°55.40′ W|
|38°47.35′ N|74°34.50′ W|

(b) A traffic lane for westbound traffic is established between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographic positions:

| Latitude  | Longitude |
|-----------|-----------|
|38°48.32′ N|74°55.30′ W|
|38°49.80′ N|74°34.60′ W|

| Latitude  | Longitude |
|-----------|-----------|
|38°45.45′ N|74°56.20′ W|
|38°44.45′ N|74°34.35′ W|
