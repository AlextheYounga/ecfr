##### § 167.452 In the Santa Barbara Channel: Between Point Conception and Point Arguello. #####

(a) A separation zone is bounded by a line connecting the following geographical positions:

| Latitude  |  Longitude  |
|-----------|-------------|
|34°20.90′ N|120°30.16′ W.|
|34°18.90′ N|120°30.96′ W.|
|34°25.70′ N|120°51.81′ W.|
|34°23.75′ N|120°52.51′ W.|

(b) A traffic lane for westbound traffic is established between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographical positions:

| Latitude  |  Longitude  |
|-----------|-------------|
|34°21.80′ N|120°29.96′ W.|
|34°26.60′ N|120°51.51′ W.|

| Latitude  |  Longitude  |
|-----------|-------------|
|34°18.00′ N|120°31.16′ W.|
|34°22.80′ N|120°52.76′ W.|
