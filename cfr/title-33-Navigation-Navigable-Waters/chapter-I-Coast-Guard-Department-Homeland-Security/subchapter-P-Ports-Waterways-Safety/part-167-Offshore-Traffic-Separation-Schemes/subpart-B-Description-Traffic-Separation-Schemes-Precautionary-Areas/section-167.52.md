##### § 167.52 In the approaches to Portland, ME: Eastern approach. #####

(a) A separation zone 1 mile wide is established and is centered upon the following geographical positions:

| Latitude  |  Longitude  |
|-----------|-------------|
|43°30.18′ N|069°59.17′ W.|
|43°24.28′ N|069°32.70′ W.|

(b) A traffic lane 1/2 miles wide is established on each side of the separation zone.
