##### ยง 120.309 What is my right of appeal? #####

Any person directly affected by a decision or action taken by the Commanding Officer, Marine Safety Center, under this part, may appeal that action or decision to the Assistant Commandant for Prevention Policy (CG-5P) according to the procedures in 46 CFR 1.03-15.
