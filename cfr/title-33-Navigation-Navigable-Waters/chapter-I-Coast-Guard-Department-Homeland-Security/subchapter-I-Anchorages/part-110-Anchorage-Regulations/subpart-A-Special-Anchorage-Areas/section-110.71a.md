##### § 110.71a Cabin Creek, Grasonville, Md. #####

The waters of Cabin Creek, Maryland, enclosed by a line drawn from latitude 38°56′34″ N., longitude 76°12′49″ W., on the western shore to latitude 38°56′28″ N., longitude 76°12′29″ W., on the eastern shore; thence following the general line of the shore to the point of beginning.
