##### ยง 6.04-6 Establishing security zones; prohibitions with respect thereto. #####

The Captain of a Port may establish security zones subject to the terms and conditions specified in ยง 6.01-5. No person or vessel shall enter a security zone without the permission of the Captain of the Port. No person shall board or take or place any article or thing on board any vessel in a security zone without the permission of the Captain of the Port. No person shall take or place any article or thing upon any waterfront facility in any such zone without such permission.
