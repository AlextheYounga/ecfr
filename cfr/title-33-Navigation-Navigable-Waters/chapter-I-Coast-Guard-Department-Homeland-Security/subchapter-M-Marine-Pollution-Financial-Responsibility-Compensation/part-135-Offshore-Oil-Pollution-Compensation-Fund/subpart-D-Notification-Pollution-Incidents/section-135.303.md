##### ยง 135.303 Definitions. #####

As used in this subpart:

*Occurrences which pose an imminent threat of oil pollution* means those incidents that are likely to result in a discharge of oil and include, but are not limited to: vessel collisions, grounding or stranding; structural failure in a tank, pipeline or other oil handling system; fire, explosion or other events which may cause structural damage to a vessel or offshore facility.
