##### § 381.403 Petitions for rate approval pursuant to § 284.123(b)(2). #####

The fee established for a petition for rate approval pursuant to § 284.123(b)(2) is $16,770. Such fee must be submitted in accordance with subpart A of this part and § 284.123(b)(2).
