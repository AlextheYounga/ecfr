##### § 381.403 Petitions for rate approval pursuant to § 284.123(b)(2). #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 469, Jan. 4, 2018.

The fee established for a petition for rate approval pursuant to § 284.123(b)(2) is $12,760. Such fee must be submitted in accordance with subpart A of this part and § 284.123(b)(2).
