##### ยง 1301.70 Purpose and scope. #####

(a) This subpart governs all Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) employees and contractors involved with all phases of developing, drafting and issuing TVA guidance documents.

(b) Subject to the qualifications and exemptions contained in this subpart, these procedures apply to all TVA guidance documents, as defined by the Administrative Procedures Act, Executive Order 13891 and the Office of Management and Budget memo M-20-02, *Memorandum for Regulatory Policy Officers at Executive Departments and Agencies and Managing and Executive Directors of Certain Agencies and Commissions,* Dominic J. Mancini, OIRA Acting Director (Oct. 31, 2019).
