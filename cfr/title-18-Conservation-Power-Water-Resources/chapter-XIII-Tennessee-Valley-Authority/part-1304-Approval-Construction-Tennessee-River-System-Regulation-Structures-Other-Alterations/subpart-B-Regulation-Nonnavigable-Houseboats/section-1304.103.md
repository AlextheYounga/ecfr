##### ยง 1304.103 Approval of plans for structural modifications or rebuilding of approved nonnavigable houseboats. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 44474, Aug. 31, 2018.

Plans for the structural modification, or rebuilding of an approved nonnavigable houseboat shall be submitted to TVA for review and approval in advance of any structural modification which would increase the length, width, height, or flotation of the structure.
