##### ยง 538.534 Third-country diplomatic and consular funds transfers. #####

U.S. depository institutions, U.S. registered brokers or dealers in securities, and U.S. registered money transmitters are authorized to process funds transfers for the operating expenses or other official business of third-country diplomatic or consular missions in Sudan, provided such transfers are not by, to, or through a person who is included within the term *Government of Sudan,* as defined in ยง 538.305.
