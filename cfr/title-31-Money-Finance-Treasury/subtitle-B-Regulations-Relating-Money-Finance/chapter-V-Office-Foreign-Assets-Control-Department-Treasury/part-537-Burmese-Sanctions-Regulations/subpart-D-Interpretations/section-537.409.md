##### § 537.409 Activities under pre-May 21, 1997 agreements. #####

Pursuant to § 537.210(d), a U.S. person who is a party to a pre-May 21, 1997 agreement may enter into subsequent agreements where such agreements are pursuant to, or in exercise of rights under, the pre-May 21, 1997 agreement and are specifically contemplated by the pre-May 21, 1997 agreement, unless such subsequent agreements involve any activity prohibited by § 537.201(a) with respect to any person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Executive Order 13448 of October 18, 2007 (E.O. 13448), Executive Order 13464 of April 30, 2008 (E.O. 13464), or Executive Order 13619 of July 11, 2012 (E.O. 13619), or by § 537.203. The exercise of rights under a pre-May 21, 1997 agreement may include the exercise of options to extend the contract, depending on such factors as the degree of specificity with which the option to extend is described in the pre-May 21, 1997 agreement and the degree to which the party wishing to renew can enforce its decision to exercise the option, unless such exercise of rights involves any activity prohibited by § 537.201(a) with respect to any person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to E.O. 13448, E.O. 13464, or E.O. 13619, or by § 537.203.
