##### ยง 592.304 Exporting authority. #####

(a) The term *exporting authority* means one or more entities designated by a Participant from whose territory a shipment of rough diamonds is being exported as having the authority to validate the Kimberley Process Certificate.

(b) The exporting authority for the United States is the U.S. Bureau of the Census.

Note to ยง 592.304.

The Secretary of State will periodically publish in the Federal Register an up-to-date listing of the exporting authorities of all Participants.
