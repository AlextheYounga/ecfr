##### § 236.251 Cross-reference. #####

All of the provisions of subpart B, part 207 of this chapter covering mortgages insured under section 207 of the National Housing Act, apply with full force and effect to mortgages insured under section 236 of the National Housing Act except the following provisions:

Sec.207.252 First, second, and third premiums.207.252a Premiums—operating loss loans.207.259 Insurance benefits.207.262 No vested right in fund.[37 FR 8664, Apr. 29, 1972, as amended at 42 FR 59675, Nov. 18, 1977]
