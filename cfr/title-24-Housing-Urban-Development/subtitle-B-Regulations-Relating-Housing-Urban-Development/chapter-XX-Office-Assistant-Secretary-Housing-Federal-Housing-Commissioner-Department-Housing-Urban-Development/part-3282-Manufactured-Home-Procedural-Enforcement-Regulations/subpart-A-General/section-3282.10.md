##### ยง 3282.10 Civil and criminal penalties. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 13045, Mar. 6, 2020.

Failure to comply with this part may subject the party in question to the civil and criminal penalties provided for in section 611 of the Act, 42 U.S.C. 5410. The maximum amount of penalties imposed under section 611 of the Act shall be $2,924 for each violation, up to a maximum of $3,654,955 for any related series of violations occurring within one year from the date of the first violation.
