##### ยง 3004.61 Freedom of Information Act Public Liaison. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 9618, Feb. 19, 2020.

The Commission designates the Director of the Office of Public Affairs and Government Relations or his or her designee as the FOIA Public Liaison who shall assist in the resolution of any dispute between a requester and the Commission. The FOIA Public Liaison may be contacted via e-mail at *PRC-PAGR@prc.gov* or telephone at 202-789-6800.
