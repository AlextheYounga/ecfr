##### ยง 111.4 Approval of the Director of the Federal Register. #####

Incorporation by reference of the publication now titled the Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual was approved by the Director of the Federal Register under 5 U.S.C 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51 on September 29, 2016.
