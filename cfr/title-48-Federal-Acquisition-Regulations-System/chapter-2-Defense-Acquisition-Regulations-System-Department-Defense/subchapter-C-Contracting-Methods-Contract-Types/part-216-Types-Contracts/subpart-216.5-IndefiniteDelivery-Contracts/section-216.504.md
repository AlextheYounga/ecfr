##### 216.504 Indefinite-quantity contracts. #####

(c)(1)(ii)(D) *Limitation on single-award contracts.* (*i*) The authority to make the determination authorized in FAR 16.504(c)(1)(ii)(D)(1) shall not be delegated below the level of the senior procurement executive.

(*ii*) A copy of each determination made in accordance with FAR 16.504(c)(1)(ii)(D) shall be submitted to the Director, Defense Procurement and Acquisition Policy, via the OUSD(AT&L)DPAP/CPIC email address at *osd.pentagon.ousd-atl.mbx.cpic@mail.mil.*
