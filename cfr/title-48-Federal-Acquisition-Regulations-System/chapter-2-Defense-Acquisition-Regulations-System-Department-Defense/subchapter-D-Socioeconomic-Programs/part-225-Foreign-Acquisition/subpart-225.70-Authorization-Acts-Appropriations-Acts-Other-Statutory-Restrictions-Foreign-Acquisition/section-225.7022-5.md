##### 225.7022-5 Solicitation provision and contract clause. #####

(a) Use the provision at 252.225-7059, Prohibition on Certain Procurements from the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region - Certification, in solicitations, including solicitations using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items and COTS items, that contain the clause at 252.225-7060, Prohibition on Certain Procurements from the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

(b) Use the clause at 252.225-7060, Prohibition on Certain Procurements from the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, in solicitations, contracts, and orders for products utilizing funds appropriated or otherwise made available for fiscal year 2022, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items and COTS items.
