##### 225.7010-4 Implementation of restriction on certain naval vessel components. #####

(a) 10 U.S.C. 4864(h) prohibits the use of contract clauses or certifications to implement the restriction at 225.7010-1(a).

(b) Agencies shall accomplish implementation of the restriction at 225.7010-1(a) through use of management and oversight techniques that achieve the objectives of this section without imposing a significant management burden on the Government or the contractor involved.
