##### 211.503 Contract clauses. #####

(b) Use the clause at FAR 52.211-12, Liquidated Damages - Construction, in all construction contracts exceeding $700,000, except cost-plus-fixed-fee contracts or contracts where the contractor cannot control the pace of the work. Use of the clause in contracts of $700,000 or less is optional.
