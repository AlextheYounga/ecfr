##### 211.275-3 Contract clause. #####

Use the clause at *252.211-7006,* Passive Radio Frequency Identification, in solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items, that will require shipment of items meeting the criteria at *211.275-2,* and complete paragraph (b)(1)(ii) of the clause at 252.211-7006 as appropriate.
