##### 232.009-2 Contract clause. #####

Link to an amendment published at 88 FR 12863, Mar. 1, 2023.

Use the clause at 252.232-7017, Accelerating Payments to Small Business Subcontractors - Prohibition on Fees and Consideration, in solicitations and contracts, including those using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, that include the clause at FAR 52.232-40, Providing Accelerated Payments to Small Business Subcontractors.
