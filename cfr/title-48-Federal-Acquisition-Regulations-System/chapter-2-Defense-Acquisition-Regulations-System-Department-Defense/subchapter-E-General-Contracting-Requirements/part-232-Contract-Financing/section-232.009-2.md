##### 232.009-2 Contract clause. #####

Use the clause at 252.232-7017, Accelerating Payments to Small Business Subcontractors - Prohibition on Fees and Consideration, in solicitations and contracts, including those using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items, that include the clause at FAR 52.232-40, Providing Accelerated Payments to Small Business Subcontractors.
