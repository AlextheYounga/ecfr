##### 3052.231-70 Precontract costs. #####

As prescribed in (HSAR) 48 CFR 3031.205-32, insert the following clause:

Precontract Costs (DEC 2003)

The Contractor shall be entitled to reimbursement for pre-contract costs incurred on or after \_\_\_ in an amount not to exceed $\_\_\_ that, if incurred after this contract had been entered into, would have been reimbursable under this contract.

(End of clause)
