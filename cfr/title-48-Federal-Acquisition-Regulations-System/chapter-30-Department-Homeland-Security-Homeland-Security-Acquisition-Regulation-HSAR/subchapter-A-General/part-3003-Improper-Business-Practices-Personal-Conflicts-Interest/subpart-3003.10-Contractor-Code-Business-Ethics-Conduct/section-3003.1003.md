##### 3003.1003 Requirements. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 17315, Apr. 2, 2021.

(a) *Contractor requirements.* Contractors making written disclosures under the clause at (FAR) 48 CFR 52.203-13 must use the electronic Contractor Disclosure Form at *http://www.oig.dhs.gov.* Contractors making disclosures under contracts which do not contain the clause at (FAR) 48 CFR 52.203-13 are encouraged to also use this electronic form.
