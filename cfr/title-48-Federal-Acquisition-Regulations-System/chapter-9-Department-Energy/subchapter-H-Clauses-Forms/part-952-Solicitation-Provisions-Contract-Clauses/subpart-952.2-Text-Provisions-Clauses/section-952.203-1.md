##### 952.203-1 Identification of contractor employees. #####

As prescribed at 903.1004, insert the following clause:

Identification of Contractor Employees [December 2024]

Contractors and their employees shall be properly identified in communications (*e.g.,* email communications, texts, video and teleconference calls, etc.) and in meetings so that all participants can differentiate between Federal employees and contractor employees.

(End of clause)[89 FR 89760, Nov. 13, 2024]
