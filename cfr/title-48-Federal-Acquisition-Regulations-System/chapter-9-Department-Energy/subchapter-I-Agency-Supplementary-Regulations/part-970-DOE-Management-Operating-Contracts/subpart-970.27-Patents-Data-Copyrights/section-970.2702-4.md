##### 970.2702-4 Royalties. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 89786, Nov. 13, 2024.

Contracting officers must use the solicitation provision at 970.5227-7, Royalty Information, and the clause at 970.5227-8, Refund of Royalties instead of the provision at 48 CFR 52.227-8 and the clause at 48 CFR 52.227-9, respectively.
