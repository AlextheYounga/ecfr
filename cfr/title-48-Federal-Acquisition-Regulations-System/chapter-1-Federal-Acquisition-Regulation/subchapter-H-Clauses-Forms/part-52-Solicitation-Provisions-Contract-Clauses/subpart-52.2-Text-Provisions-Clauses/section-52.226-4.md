##### 52.226-4 Notice of Disaster or Emergency Area Set-Aside. #####

As prescribed in 26.206(b), insert the following clause:

Notice of Disaster or Emergency Area Set-Aside (NOV 2007)

(a) *Set-aside area.* Offers are solicited only from businesses residing or primarily doing business in \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ [*Contracting Officer to fill in with definite geographic boundaries.*] Offers received from other businesses shall not be considered.

(b) This set-aside is in addition to any small business set-aside contained in this contract.

(End of clause) [72 FR 63088, Nov. 7, 2007, as amended at 74 FR 52849, Oct. 14, 2009]
