##### 52.223-17 Affirmative Procurement of EPA-designated Items in Service and Construction Contracts. #####

As prescribed in 23.406(e), insert the following clause:

Affirmative Procurement of EPA-Designated Items in Service and Construction Contracts (AUG 2018)

(a) In the performance of this contract, the Contractor shall make maximum use of products containing recovered materials that are EPA-designated items unless the product cannot be acquiredâ€”

(1) Competitively within a timeframe providing for compliance with the contract performance schedule;

(2) Meeting contract performance requirements; or

(3) At a reasonable price.

(b) Information about this requirement is available at EPA's Comprehensive Procurement Guidelines web site, *https://www.epa.gov/smm/comprehensive-procurement-guideline-cpg-program*. The list of EPA-designated items is available at *https://www.epa.gov/smm/comprehensive-procurement-guideline-cpg-program*.
