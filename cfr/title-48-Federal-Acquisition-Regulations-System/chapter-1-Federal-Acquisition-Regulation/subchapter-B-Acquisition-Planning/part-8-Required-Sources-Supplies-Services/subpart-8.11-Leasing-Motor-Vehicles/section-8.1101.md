##### 8.1101 Definitions. #####

As used in this subpart -

*Leasing,* means the acquisition of motor vehicles, other than by purchase from private or commercial sources, and includes the synonyms *hire* and *rent.*

*Motor vehicle* means an item of equipment, mounted on wheels and designed for highway and/or land use, that (1) derives power from a self-contained power unit or (2) is designed to be towed by and used in conjunction with self-propelled equipment.
