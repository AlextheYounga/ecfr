##### 46.709 Warranties of commercial items. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 61031, Nov. 4, 2021.

The contracting officer should take advantage of commercial warranties, including extended warranties, where appropriate and in the Government's best interests, offered by the contractor for the repair and replacement of commercial items (see part 12).
