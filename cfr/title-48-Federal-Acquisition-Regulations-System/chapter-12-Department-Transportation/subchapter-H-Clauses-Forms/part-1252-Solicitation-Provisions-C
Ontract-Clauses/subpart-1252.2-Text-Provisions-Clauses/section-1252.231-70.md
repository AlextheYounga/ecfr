##### 1252.231-70 Date of Incurrence of Costs. #####

As prescribed in 1231.205-3270(b), insert the following clause:

Date of Incurrence of Costs (NOV 2022)

The Contractor shall be entitled to reimbursement for costs incurred on or after\_\_ [*Contracting Officer insert date*] in an amount not to exceed $\_\_ [*Contracting Officer insert amount*] that, if incurred after this contract had been entered into, would have been reimbursable under this contract.

(End of clause)
