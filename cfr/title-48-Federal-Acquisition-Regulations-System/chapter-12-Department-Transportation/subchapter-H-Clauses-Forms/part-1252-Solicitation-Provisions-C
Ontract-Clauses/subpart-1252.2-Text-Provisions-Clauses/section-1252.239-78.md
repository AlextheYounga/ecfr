(a) The Contractor shall use only cryptographic mechanisms that comply with \_\_\_ [*Contracting Officer insert FIPS 140-2 level #*]. All deliverables shall be labeled \_\_\_ [*Contracting Officer insert appropriate label such as “For Official Use Only” (FOUO) or other DOT-agency selected designation per document sensitivity*].
