##### 1252.216-74 Settlement of Letter Contract. #####

As prescribed in 1216.603-4, insert the following clause:

Settlement of Letter Contract (NOV 2022)

(a) This contract constitutes the definitive contract contemplated by issuance of letter contract\_\_\_[*insert number*] dated\_\_\_[*insert effective date*]. It supersedes the letter contract and its modification number(s)\_\_\_[*insert number(s)*] and, to the extent of any inconsistencies, governs.

(b) The cost(s) and fee(s), or price(s), established in this definitive contract represents full and complete settlement of letter contract\_\_\_[*insert number*] and modification number(s)\_\_\_[*insert number(s)*]. Payment of the agreed upon fee or profit withheld pending definitization of the letter contract, may commence immediately at the rate and times stated within this contract.

(End of clause)
