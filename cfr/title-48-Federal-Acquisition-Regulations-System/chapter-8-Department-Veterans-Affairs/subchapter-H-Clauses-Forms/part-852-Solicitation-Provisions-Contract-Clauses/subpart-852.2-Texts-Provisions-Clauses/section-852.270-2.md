##### 852.270-2 Bread and bakery products - quantities. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 48263, Sept. 24, 2018.

As prescribed in 870.111-3, insert the following clause:

Bread and Bakery Products - Quantities (JAN 2008)

The bidder agrees to furnish up to 25 percent more or 25 percent less than the quantities awarded when ordered by the Department of Veterans Affairs.

(End of clause)
