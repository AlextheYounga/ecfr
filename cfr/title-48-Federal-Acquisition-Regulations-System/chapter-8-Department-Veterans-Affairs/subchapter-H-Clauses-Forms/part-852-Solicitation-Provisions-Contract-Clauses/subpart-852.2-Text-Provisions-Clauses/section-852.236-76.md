##### 852.236-76 Correspondence. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 9974, Mar. 19, 2019.

As prescribed in 836.570, insert the following clause:

Correspondence (APR 1984)

All correspondence relative to this contract shall bear the Specification Number, Project Number, Department of Veterans Affairs Contract Number, title of project and name of facility.

(End of clause)
