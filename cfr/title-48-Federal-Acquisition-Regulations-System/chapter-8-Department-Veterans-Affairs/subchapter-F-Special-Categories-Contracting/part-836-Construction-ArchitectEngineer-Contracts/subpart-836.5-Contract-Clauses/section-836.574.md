##### 836.574 Subcontracts and work coordination. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 9972, Mar. 19, 2019.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 852.236-80, Subcontracts and work coordination, in solicitations and contracts for construction expected to exceed the micro-purchase threshold for construction. When the solicitations or contracts are for new construction work with complex mechanical-electrical work, the contracting officer may use the clause with its Alternate I.
