##### 1352.231-70 Precontract costs. #####

Precontract Costs (APR 2010)

The contractor is entitled to reimbursement for allowable, allocable, and reasonable costs incurred during the period of \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ to the award date of this contract in an amount not to exceed $\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_.
