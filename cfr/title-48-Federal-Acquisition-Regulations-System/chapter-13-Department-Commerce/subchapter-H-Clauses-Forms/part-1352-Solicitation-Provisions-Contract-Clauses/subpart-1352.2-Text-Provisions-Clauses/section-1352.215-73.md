##### 1352.215-73 Evaluation quantities—indefinite quantity contract. #####

As prescribed in 48 CFR 1315.204-570(b)(1), insert the following provision:

Evaluation Quantities—Indefinite Quantity Contract (Date)

To evaluate offers for award purposes, the Government will apply the offeror's proposed fixed-prices/rates to the estimated quantities included in the solicitation (and add to this amount other direct costs, if applicable).

(End of clause)
