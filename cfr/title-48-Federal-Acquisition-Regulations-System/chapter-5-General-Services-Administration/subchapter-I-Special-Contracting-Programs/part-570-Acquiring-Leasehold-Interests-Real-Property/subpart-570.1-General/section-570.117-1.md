##### 570.117-1 Federal leadership in environmental, energy, and economic performance. #####

Link to an amendment published at 89 FR 63109, Aug. 2, 2024.

In order to create a clean energy economy that will increase our Nation's prosperity, promote energy security, protect the interests of taxpayers, and safeguard the health of our environment, GSA will accomplish all requirements of E.O. 13514 that apply to lease acquisition.
