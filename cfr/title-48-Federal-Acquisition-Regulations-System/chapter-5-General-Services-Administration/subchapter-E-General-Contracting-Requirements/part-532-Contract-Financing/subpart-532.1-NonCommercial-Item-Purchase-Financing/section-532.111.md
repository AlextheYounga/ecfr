##### 532.111 Contract clauses for non-commercial purchases. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 3717, Feb. 13, 2019.

For contracts that include the clause at FAR 52.232-5, Payments Under Fixed-Price Construction Contracts, the contracting officer shall provide the contractor with GSA Form 2419, Certification of Progress Payments Under Fixed-Price Construction Contracts, to be used to make the certification required by FAR 52.232-5(c).
