##### 552.204-9 Personal Identity Verification requirements. #####

Link to an amendment published at 88 FR 18075, Mar. 27, 2023.

As prescribed in 504.1303, insert the following clause:

Personal Identity Verification Requirements (JUN 2021)

(a) The contractor shall comply with GSA personal identity verification requirements, identified in the CIO P 2181.1 GSA HSPD-12 Personal Identity Verification and Credentialing Handbook, if contractor employees require access to GSA controlled facilities or information systems to perform contract requirements. The contractor can find the CIO policy and additional information at *http://www.gsa.gov/hspd12.*

(b) The Contractor shall insert this clause in all subcontracts when the subcontractor is required to have access to a GSA-controlled facility or access to a GSA-controlled information system.

(End of clause)[77 FR 59793, Oct. 1, 2012, as amended at 85 FR 38337, June 26, 2020; 86 FR 28500, May 27, 2021]
