##### 552.238-93 Order Acknowledgement. #####

As prescribed in 538.273(d)(17), insert the following clause:

Order Acknowledgement (MAY 2019)

Contractors shall acknowledge only those orders which state “Order Acknowledgement Required.” These orders shall be acknowledged within 10 calendar days after receipt. Such acknowledgement shall be sent to the ordering activity placing the order and contain information pertinent to the order, including the anticipated delivery date.

(End of clause)[84 FR 17046, Apr. 23, 2019]
