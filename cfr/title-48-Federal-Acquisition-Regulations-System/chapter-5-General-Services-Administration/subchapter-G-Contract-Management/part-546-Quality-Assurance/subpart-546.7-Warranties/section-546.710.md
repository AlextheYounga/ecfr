##### 546.710 Contract clause. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 55521, Oct. 6, 2021.

The Contracting officer shall insert the clause at 552.246-77, Additional Contract Warranty Provisions for Supplies of a Noncomplex Nature, when using the clause at 52.246-17 in solicitations and contracts.
