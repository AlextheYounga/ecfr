##### 452.215-73 Post Award Conference. #####

As prescribed in 415.570, insert a clause substantially as follows:

Post Award Conference (NOV 1996)

A post award conference with the successful offeror is required. It will be scheduled within \_\_\_\_\_\_\* days after the date of contract award. The conference will be held at: \_\_\_\_\_\_\*.

(End of clause)

\* *Contracting officer to insert number of days and location.*
