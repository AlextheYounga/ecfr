##### ยง 81.10 Who may cast a vote in a Secretarial election? #####

|                                        If the tribe:                                        |                                                                                                                                       Then the following individuals may cast a vote:                                                                                                                                        |
|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|                (a) Is reorganizing under Federal statute for the first time,                |                                 Any member of the tribe who:  <br/>(1) Will be 18 years of age or older on the date of the Secretarial election; and  <br/>(2) Has duly registered, regardless of residence or other qualifications contained in the tribe's governing documents or charter                                  |
|                      (b) Is already reorganized under Federal statute,                      |        Any member of the tribe who:  <br/>(1) Will be 18 years of age or older on the date of the Secretarial election; and  <br/>(2) Otherwise meets the qualifications required by the tribe's governing documents or charter for that particular type of Secretarial election; and  <br/>(3) Has duly registered.         |
|(c) Is not reorganized under a Federal statute but tribal law requires a Secretarial election|Any member of the tribe who:  <br/>(1) Will be 18 years of age or older on the date of the Secretarial election; and  <br/>(2) Otherwise meets the qualifications, if any, required by the tribe's governing documents or charter for that particular type of Secretarial election, if any; and  <br/>(3) Has duly registered.|
