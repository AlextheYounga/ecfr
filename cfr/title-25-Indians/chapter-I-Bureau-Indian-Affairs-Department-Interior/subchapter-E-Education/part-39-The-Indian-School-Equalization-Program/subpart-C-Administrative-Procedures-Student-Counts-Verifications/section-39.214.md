##### ยง 39.214 What is the minimum number of instructional hours required in order to be considered a full-time educational program? #####

A full time program provides the following number of instructional/student hours to the corresponding grade level:

|Grade|Hours|
|-----|-----|
|  K  | 720 |
| 1-3 | 810 |
| 4-8 | 900 |
|9-12 | 970 |
