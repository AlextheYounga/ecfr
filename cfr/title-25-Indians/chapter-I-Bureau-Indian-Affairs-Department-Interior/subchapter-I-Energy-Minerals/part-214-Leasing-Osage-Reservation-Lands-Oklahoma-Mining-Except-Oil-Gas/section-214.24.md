##### ยง 214.24 Forms. #####

Applications, leases, and other papers must be upon forms prepared by the department, and the superintendent of the Osage Indian school, Pawhuska, Okla., will furnish prospective lessees with such forms at a cost of $1 per set.

Form M. Application for mining lease, including financial showing.Form N. Lease (except lead and zinc).Form O. Bond.Form P. Authority of officers to execute papers.Form Q. Assignment.Form R. Lease for lead and zinc.Form S. Collective bond.
