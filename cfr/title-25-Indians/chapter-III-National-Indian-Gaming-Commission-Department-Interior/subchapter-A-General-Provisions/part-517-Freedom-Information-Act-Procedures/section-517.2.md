##### ยง 517.2 Public reading room. #####

Records that are required to be maintained by the Commission shall be available for public inspection and copying at 90 K Street NE., Suite 200, Washington, DC 20002. Reading room records created on or after November 1, 1996, shall be made available electronically via the Web site.
