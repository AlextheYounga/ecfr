##### ยง 514.8 Where should fees, quarterly statements, and other communications about fees be sent? #####

The statements, remittances, and communications about fees shall be transmitted to the Commission at the following address: NIGC Attn: Comptroller, C/O Department of the Interior, 1849 C Street NW., Mail Stop #1621, Washington, DC 20240. Checks should be made payable to the National Indian Gaming Commission (do not remit cash).
