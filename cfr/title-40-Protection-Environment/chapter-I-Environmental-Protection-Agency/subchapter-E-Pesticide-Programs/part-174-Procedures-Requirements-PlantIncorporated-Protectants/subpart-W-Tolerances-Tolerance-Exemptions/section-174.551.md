##### ยง 174.551 Brevibacillus laterosporus Mpp75Aa1.1 protein; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance. #####

Residues of *Brevibacillus laterosporus* Mpp75Aa1.1 protein in or on the food and feed commodities of corn: corn, field; corn, sweet; and corn, pop are exempt from the requirement of a tolerance when used as a plant-incorporated protectant in corn.
