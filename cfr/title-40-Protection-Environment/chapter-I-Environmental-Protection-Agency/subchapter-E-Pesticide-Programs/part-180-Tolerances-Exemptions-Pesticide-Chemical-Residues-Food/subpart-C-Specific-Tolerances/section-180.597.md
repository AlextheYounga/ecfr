| Cattle, meat byproducts  |      0.01       |

|Grain, aspirated fractions|      0.60       |
|  Horse, meat byproducts  |      0.01       |
|  Sheep, meat byproducts  |      0.01       |
|      Wheat, forage       |      0.60       |
|       Wheat, germ        |      0.10       |
|       Wheat, grain       |      0.03       |
|        Wheat, hay        |      0.06       |
|       Wheat, straw       |      0.30       |
