##### ยง 180.592 Butafenacil; tolerances for residues. #####

(a) *General.* (1) Tolerances are established for residues of the herbicide butafenacil, (1,1-dimethyl-2-oxo-2-(2-propenyloxy)ethyl 2-chloro-5-[3,6-dihydro-3-methyl-2,6-dioxo-4-(trifluoromethyl)-1(2*H*)-pyrimidinyl] benzoate) in or on the following raw agricultural commodities:

|       Commodity       |Parts per million|
|-----------------------|-----------------|
|Cotton, gin byproducts |       10        |
|Cotton, undelinted seed|      0.50       |
