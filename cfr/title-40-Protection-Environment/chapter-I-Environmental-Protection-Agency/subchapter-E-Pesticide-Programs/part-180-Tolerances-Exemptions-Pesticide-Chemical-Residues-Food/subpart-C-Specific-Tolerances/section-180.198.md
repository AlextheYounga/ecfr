##### ยง 180.198 Trichlorfon; tolerances for residues. #####

(a) *General.* Tolerances are established for residues of the insecticide trichlorfon (dimethyl (2,2,2-trichloro-1-hydroxyethyl) phosphonate) in or on the following food commodities:

|                                       Commodity                                        |Parts per million|
|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
|                                Cattle, fat <sup>1</sup>                                |       0.5       |
|                               Cattle, meat <sup>1</sup>                                |       0.2       |
|                          Cattle, meat byproducts <sup>1</sup>                          |       0.1       |
|<sup>1</sup> There are no U.S. registrations for cattle commodities as of June 24, 1999.|                 |
