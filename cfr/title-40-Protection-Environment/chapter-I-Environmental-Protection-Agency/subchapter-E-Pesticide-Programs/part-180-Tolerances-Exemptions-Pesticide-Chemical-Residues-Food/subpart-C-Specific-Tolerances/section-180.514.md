##### ยง 180.514 Cloransulam-methyl; tolerances for residues. #####

|   Commodity   |Parts per million|
|---------------|-----------------|
|Soybean, forage|       0.1       |
| Soybean, hay  |       0.2       |
| Soybean, seed |      0.02       |
