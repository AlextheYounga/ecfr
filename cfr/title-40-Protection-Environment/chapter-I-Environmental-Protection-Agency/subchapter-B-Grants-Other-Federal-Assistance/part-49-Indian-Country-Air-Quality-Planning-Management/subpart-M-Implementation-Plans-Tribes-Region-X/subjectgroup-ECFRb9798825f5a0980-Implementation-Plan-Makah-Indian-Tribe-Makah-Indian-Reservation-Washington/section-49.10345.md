##### ยง 49.10345 Classification of regions for episode plans. #####

The air quality control region which encompasses the Makah Indian Reservation is classified as follows for purposes of episode plans:

|        Pollutant        |Classification|
|-------------------------|--------------|
|     Carbon monoxide     |     III      |
|    Nitrogen dioxide     |     III      |
|          Ozone          |     III      |
|Particulate matter (PM10)|      II      |
|      Sulfur oxides      |      II      |
