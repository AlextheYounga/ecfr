##### § 422.65 Standards of performance for new sources. #####

The following limitations establish the quantity or quality of pollutants or pollutant properties, controlled by this section, which may be discharged by a point source subject to the provisions of this subpart after application of the standards of performance for new sources:

|         Effluent characteristic         |                      Effluent limitations                       |              |
|-----------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|--------------|
|          Maximum for any 1 day          |Average of daily values for 30 consecutive days shall not exceed—|              |
|                   TSS                   |                              0.35                               |     0.18     |
|         Total phosphorus (as P)         |                               .56                               |     .28      |
|             Fluoride (as F)             |                               .21                               |     .11      |
|                   pH                    |                         (<sup>1</sup>)                          |(<sup>1</sup>)|
|<sup>1</sup> Within the range 6.0 to 9.5.|                                                                 |              |
