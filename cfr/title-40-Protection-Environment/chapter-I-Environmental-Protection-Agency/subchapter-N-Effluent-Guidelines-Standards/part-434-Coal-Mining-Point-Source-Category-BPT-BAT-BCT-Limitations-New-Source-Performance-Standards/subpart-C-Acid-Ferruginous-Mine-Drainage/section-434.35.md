##### § 434.35 New source performance standards (NSPS). #####

Except as provided in 40 CFR 401.17, and §§ 434.61, 434.62 and 434.63 of this part, the following new source performance standards shall be achieved for any discharge from a new source subject to this subpart:

|           Pollutant or pollutant property            |Maximum for any 1 day|Average of daily values for 30 consecutive days|
|------------------------------------------------------|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
|                                                      |Concentration in mg/l|                                               |
|                     Iron, total                      |         6.0         |                      3.0                      |
|                   Manganese, total                   |         4.0         |                      2.0                      |
|                         TSS                          |        70.0         |                     35.0                      |
|                          pH                          |   (<sup>1</sup>)    |                (<sup>1</sup>)                 |
|<sup>1</sup> Within the range 6.0 to 9.0 at all times.|                     |                                               |
