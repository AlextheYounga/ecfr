|             Oil and grease              |                               2.0                               |     0.73     |
|             BOD<sub>5</sub>             |                              3.72                               |     2.20     |
|                   TSS                   |                              6.96                               |     2.90     |
|                   pH                    |                         (<sup>1</sup>)                          |(<sup>1</sup>)|
|                                         |           English units (lb/1,000 lb of raw material)           |              |
|             Oil and grease              |                               2.0                               |     0.73     |
|             BOD<sub>5</sub>             |                              3.72                               |     2.20     |
|                   TSS                   |                              6.96                               |     2.90     |
|                   pH                    |                         (<sup>1</sup>)                          |(<sup>1</sup>)|
|<sup>1</sup> Within the range 6.0 to 9.0.|                                                                 |              |
