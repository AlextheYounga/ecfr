##### § 410.25 New source performance standards (NSPS). #####

Any new source subject to this subpart must achieve the following new source performance standards (NSPS):

|                                           COD                                           |                     113.8                     |     73.3     |
|                                           TSS                                           |                     32.3                      |     14.4     |
|                                         Sulfide                                         |                     0.28                      |     0.14     |
|                                         Phenols                                         |                     0.14                      |     0.07     |
|                                     Total Chromium                                      |                     0.14                      |     0.07     |
