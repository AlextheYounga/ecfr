##### ยง 432.63 Effluent limitations attainable by the application of the best available technology economically achievable (BAT). #####

Except as provided in 40 CFR 125.30 through 125.32, any existing point source subject to this subpart must achieve the following effluent limitations representing the application of BAT:

(a) Facilities that generate no more than 50 million pounds per year of finished products must achieve the following effluent limitations:

|Regulated  <br/>parameter|Maximum daily <sup>1</sup>|Maximum monthly avg. <sup>1</sup>|
|-------------------------|--------------------------|---------------------------------|
|     Ammonia (as N)      |           8.0            |               4.0               |
|<sup>1</sup> mg/L (ppm). |                          |                                 |

(b) Facilities that generate more than 50 million pounds per year of finished products must achieve the following effluent limitations:

|Regulated  <br/>parameter|Maximum daily <sup>1</sup>|Maximum monthly avg. <sup>1</sup>|
|-------------------------|--------------------------|---------------------------------|
|     Ammonia (as N)      |           8.0            |               4.0               |
|     Total Nitrogen      |           194            |               134               |
|<sup>1</sup> mg/L (ppm). |                          |                                 |
