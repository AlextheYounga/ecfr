The requirements of this part apply to the chemicals and chemical categories listed in this section. This section contains five listings. Paragraph (a) of this section is an alphabetical order listing of those chemicals that have an associated Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) Registry number. Paragraph (b) of this section contains a CAS number order list of the same chemicals listed in paragraph (a) of this section. Paragraph (c) of this section contains the chemical categories for which reporting is required. These chemical categories are listed in alphabetical order and do not have CAS numbers. Paragraph (d) of this section is an alphabetical order listing of the per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances and their associated CAS Registry number. Paragraph (e) of this section contains a CAS number order list of the same chemicals listed in paragraph (d) of this section. Each listing identifies the effective date for reporting under ยง 372.30.
