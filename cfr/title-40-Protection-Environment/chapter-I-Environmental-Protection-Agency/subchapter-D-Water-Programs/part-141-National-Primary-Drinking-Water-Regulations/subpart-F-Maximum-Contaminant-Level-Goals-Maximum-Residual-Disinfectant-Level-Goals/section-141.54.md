##### ยง 141.54 Maximum residual disinfectant level goals for disinfectants. #####

MRDLGs for disinfectants are as follows:

|Disinfectant residual|      MRDLG(mg/L)       |
|---------------------|------------------------|
|      Chlorine       |4 (as Cl <sub>2</sub>). |
|     Chloramines     |4 (as Cl <sub>2</sub>). |
|  Chlorine dioxide   |0.8 (as ClO<sub>2</sub>)|
