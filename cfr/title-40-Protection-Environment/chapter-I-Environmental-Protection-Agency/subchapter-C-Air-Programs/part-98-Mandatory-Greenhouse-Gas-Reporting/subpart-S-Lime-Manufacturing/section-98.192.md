##### ยง 98.192 GHGs to report. #####

You must report:

(a) CO2 process emissions from lime kilns.

(b) CO2 emissions from fuel combustion at lime kilns.

(c) N2O and CH4 emissions from fuel combustion at each lime kiln. You must report these emissions under 40 CFR part 98, subpart C (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources).

(d) CO2, N2O, and CH4 emissions from each stationary fuel combustion unit other than lime kilns. You must report these emissions under 40 CFR part 98, subpart C (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources).

(e) CO2 collected and transferred off site under 40 CFR part 98, following the requirements of subpart PP of this part (Suppliers of Carbon Dioxide (CO2)).
