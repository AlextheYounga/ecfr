##### § 96.3 Measurements, abbreviations, and acronyms. #####

Measurements, abbreviations, and acronyms used in this part are defined as follows:

Btu—British thermal unit.hr—hour.Kwh—kilowatt hour.lb—pounds.mmBtu—million Btu.MWe—megawatt electrical.ton—2000 pounds.CO2—carbon dioxide.NOX—nitrogen oxides.O2—oxygen.
