##### ยง 191.0 Scope. #####

This part sets forth general provisions applicable to all drawback claims and specialized provisions applicable to specific types of drawback claims. Additional drawback provisions relating to the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) are contained in subpart E of part 181 of this chapter.
