##### ยง 111.72 Dismissal subject to new proceedings. #####

Link to an amendment published at 87 FR 63320, Oct. 18, 2022.

If the Secretary of Homeland Security, or his designee, finds that the evidence produced at the hearing indicates that a proper disposition of the case cannot be made on the basis of the charges preferred, he may instruct the port director to serve appropriate charges as a basis for new proceedings to be conducted in accordance with the procedures set forth in this subpart.
