##### ยง 111.56 Review of report on investigation. #####

Link to an amendment published at 87 FR 63319, Oct. 18, 2022.

The port director will review the report of investigation to determine if there is sufficient basis to recommend that charges be preferred against the broker. He will then submit his recommendation with supporting reasons to the Assistant Commissioner for final determination together with a proposed statement of charges when recommending that charges be preferred.
