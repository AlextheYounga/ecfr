##### ยง 111.55 Investigation of complaints. #####

Link to an amendment published at 87 FR 63319, Oct. 18, 2022.

Every complaint or charge against a broker which may be the basis for disciplinary action will be forwarded for investigation to the special agent in charge of the area in which the broker is located. The special agent in charge will submit a report on the investigation to the director of the port and send a copy of it to the Assistant Commissioner.
