##### § 1.336-5 Effective/applicability dates. #####

Link to an amendment published at 87 FR 324, Jan. 4, 2022.

The provisions of §§ 1.336-1 through 1.336-4 apply to any qualified stock disposition for which the disposition date is on or after May 15, 2013. The provisions of § 1.336-1(b)(5)(i)(A) relating to section 1022 are effective on and after January 19, 2017.
