##### ยง 300.12 Fee for obtaining a preparer tax identification number. #####

(a) *Applicability.* This section applies to the application for and renewal of a preparer tax identification number pursuant to 26 CFR 1.6109-2(d).

(b) *Fee.* The fee to apply for or renew a preparer tax identification number is $21 per year and is in addition to the fee charged by the contractor.

(c) *Person liable for the fee.* The individual liable for the application or renewal fee is the individual applying for and renewing a preparer tax identification number from the IRS.

(d) *Applicability date.* This section applies to applications for or renewal of a preparer tax identification number filed on or after August 17, 2020.
