##### § 54.9816-1T Basis and scope (temporary). #####

(a) *Basis.* This section and §§ 54.9816-2T through 54.9816-7T, 54.9817-1T, and 54.9822-1T implement subchapter B of chapter 100 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.

(b) *Scope.* This part establishes standards for group health plans with respect to surprise medical bills, transparency in health care coverage, and additional patient protections.
