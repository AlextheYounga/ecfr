##### § 54.9833-1 Effective dates. #####

Sections 54.9801-1 through 54.9801-6, 54.9831-1, and this section are applicable for plan years beginning on or after July 1, 2005. Notwithstanding the previous sentence, the definition of “short-term, limited-duration insurance” in § 54.9801-2 and paragraph (c)(5)(i)(C) of § 54.9831-1 apply for plan years beginning on or after January 1, 2017.
