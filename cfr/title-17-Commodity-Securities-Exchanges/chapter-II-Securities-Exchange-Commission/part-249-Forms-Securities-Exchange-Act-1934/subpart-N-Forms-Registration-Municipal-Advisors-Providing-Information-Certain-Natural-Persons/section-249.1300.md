##### ยง 249.1300 Form MA, for registration as a municipal advisor, and for amendments to registration. #####

The form shall be used for registration as a municipal advisor pursuant to section 15B of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78*o*-4) and for amendments to registrations.
