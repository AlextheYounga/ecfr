##### ยง 239.17b Form N-4, registration statement for separate accounts organized as unit investment trusts. #####

Form N-4 shall be used for registration under the Securities Act of 1933 of securities of separate accounts that offer variable annuity contracts and which register under the Investment Company Act of 1940 as unit investment trusts, and certain other separate accounts. This form is also to be used for the registration statement of such separate accounts pursuant to section 8(b) of the Investment Company Act of 1940 (ยง 274.11c of this chapter).
