##### ยง 240.12a-11 Exemption of security-based swaps sold in reliance on Securities Act of 1933 Rule 240 (ยง 230.240) from section 12(a) of the Act. #####

(a) The provisions of Section 12(a) of the Act (15 U.S.C. 78l(a)) do not apply to any security-based swap offered and sold in reliance on Rule 240 under the Securities Act of 1933.

(b) This rule will expire on February 11, 2017. In such event, the Commission will publish a rule removing this section from 17 CFR part 240 or modifying it as appropriate.
