##### ยง 249b.102 Form TA-2, 1 form to be used by transfer agents registered pursuant to section 17A of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 for the annual report of transfer agent activities. #####

1 Copies of the form may be obtained from the Publication Section, Securities and Exchange Commission, 100 F Street, NE., Washington, DC 20549 and from each of the Commission's regional offices.

This form shall be used on an annual basis for registered transfer agents for reporting their business activities.
