##### ยง 37.1305 Liquidity of financial resources. #####

The financial resources allocated by the swap execution facility to meet the requirements of ยง 37.1301 shall include unencumbered, liquid financial assets (*i.e.,* cash and/or highly liquid securities) equal to at least six months' operating costs.
