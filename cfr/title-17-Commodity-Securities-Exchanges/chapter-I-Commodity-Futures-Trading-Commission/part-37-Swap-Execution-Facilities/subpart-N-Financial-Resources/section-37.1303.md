##### ยง 37.1303 Computation of projected operating costs to meet financial resource requirement. #####

A swap execution facility shall, each fiscal quarter, make a reasonable calculation of its projected operating costs over a twelve-month period in order to determine the amount needed to meet the requirements of ยง 37.1301. The swap execution facility shall have reasonable discretion in determining the methodology used to compute such projected operating costs.
