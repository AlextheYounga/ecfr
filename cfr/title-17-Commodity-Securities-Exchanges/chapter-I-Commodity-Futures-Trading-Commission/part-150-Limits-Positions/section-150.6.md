##### ยง 150.6 Responsibility of contract markets. #####

Link to an amendment published at 86 FR 3472, Jan. 14, 2021.

Nothing in this part shall be construed to affect any provisions of the Act relating to manipulation or corners nor to relieve any contract market or its governing board from responsibility under section 5(4) of the Act to prevent manipulation and corners.
