##### § 9.3 Provisions referenced. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 1546, January 12, 2018.

Except as otherwise provided in this part, the following provisions of the Commission's rules relating to reparations contained in part 12 of this chapter apply to this part: § 12.3 (Business address; hours); § 12.5 (Computation of time); § 12.6 (Extensions of time; adjournments; postponements); § 12.7 (Ex parte communications); and § 12.12 (Signature).
