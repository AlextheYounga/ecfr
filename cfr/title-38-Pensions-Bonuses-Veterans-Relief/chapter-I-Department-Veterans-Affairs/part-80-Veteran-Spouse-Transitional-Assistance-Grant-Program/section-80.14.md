##### ยง 80.14 Compliance review requirements. #####

(a) *Site visits.* VA will conduct, as needed, site visits to grantee locations to review grantee accomplishments and management control systems.

(b) *Inspections.* VA will conduct, as needed, inspections of grantee records to determine compliance with the provisions of this part. All visits and evaluations will be performed with minimal disruption to the grantee to the extent practicable.

(Authority: Pub. L. 116-315, sec. 4304; 2 CFR 200.203)
