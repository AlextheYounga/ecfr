##### ยง 80.16 Recordkeeping. #####

Grantees must ensure that records are maintained in accordance with 2 CFR 200.337. Grantees must produce such records at VA's request.

(Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 2900-0930 (Recordkeeping))(Authority: Pub. L. 116-315, sec. 4304; 2 CFR 200.337)
