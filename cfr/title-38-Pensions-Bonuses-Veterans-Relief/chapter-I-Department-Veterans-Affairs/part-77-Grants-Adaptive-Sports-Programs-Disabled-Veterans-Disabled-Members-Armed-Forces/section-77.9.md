##### ยง 77.9 Use of pre-applications. #####

VA may request pre-applications for competitive and noncompetitive grant applications. Such request would be made in a NOFA.

(OMB has approved the information collection requirements in this section under control numbers 2900-0820, 4040-0004 for Standard Form 424, and 4040-0008 for Standard Form 424C)[79 FR 37217, July 1, 2014, as amended at 80 FR 25235, May 4, 2015]
