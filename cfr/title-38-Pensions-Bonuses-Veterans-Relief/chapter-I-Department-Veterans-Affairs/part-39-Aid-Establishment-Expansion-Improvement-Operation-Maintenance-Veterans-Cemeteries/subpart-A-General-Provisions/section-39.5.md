##### ยง 39.5 Submission of information and documents to VA. #####

All information and documents required to be submitted to VA must be submitted to the Director of the Veterans Cemetery Grants Service, National Cemetery Administration, Department of Veterans Affairs, 810 Vermont Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20420. All forms cited in this part are available at *http://www.cem.va.gov/cem/scg\_grants.asp.*
