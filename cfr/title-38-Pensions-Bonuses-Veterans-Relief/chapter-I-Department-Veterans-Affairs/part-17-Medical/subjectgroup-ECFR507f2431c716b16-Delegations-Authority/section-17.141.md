##### ยง 17.141 Authority to adjudicate foreign reimbursement claims. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 29448, June 25, 2018.

The Health Administration Center in Denver, CO, shall adjudicate claims for the payment or reimbursement of the expenses of services not previously authorized rendered in any foreign country except the Republic of the Philippines which will be referred to the VA Outpatient Clinic in Pasay City.
