##### ยง 38.775 Compliance review requirements. #####

(a) *Site visits.* VA may conduct, as needed, site visits to recipient locations to review recipient accomplishments and management control systems.

(b) *Inspections.* VA may conduct, as needed, inspections of recipient records to determine compliance with the provisions of this part. All visits and evaluations will be performed with minimal disruption to the recipient to the extent practicable.
