##### ยง 1008.18 Amendment of records. #####

Link to an amendment published at 83 FR 50837, October 10, 2018.

The Privacy Act permits individuals to request amendment of records pertaining to them if they believe the records are not accurate, relevant, timely or complete. 5 U.S.C. 552a(d)(2). A request for amendment of a record shall be submitted in accordance with the procedures in this part.
