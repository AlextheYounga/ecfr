##### § 1155.1 Purpose. #####

(a) *General.* This part implements Section 4 of Executive Order 13891 by establishing requirements and procedures for issuance, publication, and modification or withdrawal of guidance documents of the United States Access Board (“Access Board” or “agency”).

(b) *Application.* Unless otherwise stated, the requirements and procedures set forth in this part apply to all guidance documents issued or revised by the Access Board on or after October 21, 2020.
