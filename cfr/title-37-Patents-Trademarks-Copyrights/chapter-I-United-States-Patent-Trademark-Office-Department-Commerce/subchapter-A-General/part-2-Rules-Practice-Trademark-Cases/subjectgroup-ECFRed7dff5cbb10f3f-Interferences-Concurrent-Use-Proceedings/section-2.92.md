##### ยง 2.92 Preliminary to interference. #####

Link to an amendment published at 81 FR 69971, October 7, 2016.

An interference which has been declared by the Director will not be instituted by the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board until the Examiner of Trademarks has determined that the marks which are to form the subject matter of the controversy are registrable, and all of the marks have been published in the*Official Gazette* for opposition.
