##### ยง 551.80 How do I provide a registered broker-dealer confirmation? #####

(a) If you elect to satisfy ยง 551.70 by providing the customer with a registered broker-dealer confirmation, you must provide the confirmation by having the registered broker-dealer send the confirmation directly to the customer or by sending a copy of the registered broker-dealer's confirmation to the customer within one business day after you receive it.
