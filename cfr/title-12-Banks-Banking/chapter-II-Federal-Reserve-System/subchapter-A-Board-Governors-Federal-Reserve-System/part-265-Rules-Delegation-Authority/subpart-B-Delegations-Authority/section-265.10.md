##### ยง 265.10 Functions delegated to the Director of the Division of Monetary Affairs. #####

The Director of the Division of Monetary Affairs (or the Director's delegatee) is authorized:

(a) *Term Deposit Facility (TDF) test operations.* With the concurrence of the General Counsel, and in consultation with the Chair if feasible, to adjust the terms and conditions of individual TDF test operations that raise significant technical or operational issues, including but not limited to the authority to:

(1) Delay the open of a TDF operation;

(2) Extend the close of a TDF operation;

(3) Reschedule a TDF operation; and

(4) Delay the announcement of TDF operation results.

(b) *Regulation D.* With the concurrence of the General Counsel, to approve the annual indexation of the reserve requirement exemption, low reserve tranche, nonexempt deposit cutoff, and reduced reporting limit amounts under Regulation D (12 CFR part 204), so long as no change is proposed to any of the formulas by which these amounts are calculated.
