##### ยง 252.32 Risk-based and leverage capital and stress test requirements. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 59103, Nov. 1, 2019.

A bank holding company with total consolidated assets of $50 billion or more must comply with, and hold capital commensurate with the requirements of, any regulations adopted by the Board relating to capital planning and stress tests, in accordance with the applicability provisions set forth therein.
