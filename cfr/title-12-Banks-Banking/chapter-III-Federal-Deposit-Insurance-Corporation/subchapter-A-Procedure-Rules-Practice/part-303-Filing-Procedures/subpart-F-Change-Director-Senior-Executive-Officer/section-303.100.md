##### ยง 303.100 Scope. #####

Link to an amendment published at 85 FR 3244, Jan. 21, 2020.

This subpart sets forth the circumstances under which an insured state nonmember bank must notify the FDIC of a change in any member of its board of directors or any senior executive officer and the procedures for filing such notice. This subpart implements section 32 of the FDI Act (12 U.S.C. 1831i).
