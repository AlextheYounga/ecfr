##### ยง 303.230 What will the FDIC do if the application is denied? #####

(a) The FDIC will inform the applicant in writing that the application has been denied and summarize or cite the relevant considerations specified in ยง 303.229 of this subpart.

(b) The denial will also notify the applicant that a written request for a hearing under 12 CFR part 308, subpart M, may be filed with the Executive Secretary within 60 days after the denial.
