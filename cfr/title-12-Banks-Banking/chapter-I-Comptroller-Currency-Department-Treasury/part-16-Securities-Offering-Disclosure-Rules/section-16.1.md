##### ยง 16.1 Authority, purpose, and scope. #####

(a) *Authority.* This part is issued under the general authority of the national banking laws, 12 U.S.C. 1 *et seq.,* and the OCC's general rulemaking authority in 12 U.S.C. 93a.

(b) *Purpose.* This part sets forth rules governing the offer and sale of securities issued by a bank.

(c) *Scope.* This part applies to offers and sales of bank securities by issuers, underwriters, and dealers.
