##### ยง 922.1 Applicability of regulations. #####

Link to an amendment published at 84 FR 32601, July 8, 2019.

Unless noted otherwise, the regulations in Subparts A, D and E apply to all thirteen National Marine Sanctuaries for which site-specific regulations appear in Subparts F through R, respectively. Subparts B and C apply to the site evaluation list and to the designation of future Sanctuaries.
