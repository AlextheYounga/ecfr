##### ยง 30.46 Requirements for the filing of export information by pipeline carriers. #####

Link to an amendment published at 82 FR 18392, Apr. 19, 2017.

The operator of a pipeline may transport goods to a foreign country without the prior filing of the proof of filing citations, exemption, or exclusion legends, on the condition that within four calendar days following the end of each calendar month the operator will deliver to CBP Port Director the proof of filing citations, exemption, or exclusion legends covering all exports through the pipeline to each consignee during the month.
