##### ยง 30.35 Procedure for shipments exempt from filing requirements. #####

Except as noted in ยง 30.2(a)(1)(iv), where an exemption from the filing requirement is provided in this subpart of this part, a legend describing the basis for the exemption shall be made on the first page of the bill of lading, air waybill, or other commercial loading document, and on the carrier's outbound manifest.
