##### ยง 864.8175 Calibrator for platelet counting. #####

(a) *Identification.* A calibrator for platelet counting is a device that resembles platelets in plasma or whole blood and that is used to set a platelet counting instrument. It is a suspension of particles or cells whose size, shape concentration, and other characteristics have been precisely and accurately determined.

(b) *Classification.* Class II (performance standards).
