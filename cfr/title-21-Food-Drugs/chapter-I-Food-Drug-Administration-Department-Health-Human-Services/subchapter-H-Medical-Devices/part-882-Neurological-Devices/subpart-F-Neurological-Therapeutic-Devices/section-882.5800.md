##### ยง 882.5800 Cranial electrotherapy stimulator. #####

(a) *Identification.* A cranial electrotherapy stimulator is a device that applies electrical current to a patient's head to treat insomnia, depression, or anxiety.

(b) *Classification.* Class III (premarket approval).

(c) *Date a PMA or notice of completion of a PDP is required.* No effective date has been established of the requirement for premarket approval. See ยง 882.3.
