##### § 610.64 Name and address of distributor. #####

The name and address of the distributor of a product may appear on the label provided that the name, address, and license number of the manufacturer also appears on the label and the name of the distributor is qualified by one of the following phrases: “Manufactured for \_\_\_\_\_”, “Distributed by \_\_\_\_\_\_”, “Manufactured by \_\_\_\_\_ for \_\_\_\_\_”, “Manufactured for \_\_\_\_\_ by \_\_\_\_”, “Distributor: \_\_\_\_\_”, or “Marketed by \_\_\_\_\_”. The qualifying phrases may be abbreviated.
