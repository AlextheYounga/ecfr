##### § 102.47 Bonito. #####

“Bonito” or “bonito fish” is the common or usual name of the following food fishes:

*Cybiosarda elegans* (Whitely, 1935)—Leaping bonito*Gymnosarda unicolor* (Ruppell, 1838)—Dogtooth tuna*Orcynopsis unicolor* (Geoffroy St. Hilaire, 1817)—Plain bonito*Sarda australis* (Macleay, 1880)—Australian bonito*Sarda chiliensis* (Cuvier, 1831)—Eastern Pacific bonito*Sarda orientalis* (Temminck and Schlegel, 1844)—Striped bonito*Sarda sarda* (Bloch, 1793)—Atlantic bonito[55 FR 45797, Oct. 31, 1990]
