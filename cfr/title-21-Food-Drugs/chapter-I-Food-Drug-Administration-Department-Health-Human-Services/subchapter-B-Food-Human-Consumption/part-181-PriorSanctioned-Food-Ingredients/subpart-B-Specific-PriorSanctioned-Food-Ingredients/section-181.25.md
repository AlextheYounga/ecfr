##### ยง 181.25 Driers. #####

Substances classified as driers, when migrating from food-packaging material shall include:

Cobalt caprylate.Cobalt linoleate.Cobalt naphthenate.Cobalt tallate.Iron caprylate.Iron linoleate.Iron naphthenate.Iron tallate.Manganese caprylate.Manganese linoleate.Manganese naphthenate.Manganese tallate.[42 FR 14638, Mar. 15, 1977; 42 FR 56728, Oct. 28, 1977]
