##### ยง 181.23 Antimycotics. #####

Substances classified as antimycotics, when migrating from food-packaging material shall include:

Calcium propionate.Methylparaben (methyl *p*-hydroxybenzoate).Propylparaben (propyl *p*-hydroxybenzoate).Sodium benzoate.Sodium propionate.Sorbic acid.[42 FR 14638, Mar. 15, 1977; 42 FR 56728, Oct. 28, 1977]
