(a) *Specifications.* Type A medicated article containing 325 grams neomycin sulfate per pound.

(b) *Sponsor.* See No. 054771 in ยง 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) *Related tolerances.* See ยง 556.430 of this chapter.

(d) *Special considerations.* (1) Federal law restricts medicated feed containing this veterinary feed directive (VFD) drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian. See ยง 558.6 for additional requirements.

(2) The expiration date of VFDs for neomycin medicated feeds must not exceed 6 months from the date of issuance. VFDs for neomycin shall not be refilled.
