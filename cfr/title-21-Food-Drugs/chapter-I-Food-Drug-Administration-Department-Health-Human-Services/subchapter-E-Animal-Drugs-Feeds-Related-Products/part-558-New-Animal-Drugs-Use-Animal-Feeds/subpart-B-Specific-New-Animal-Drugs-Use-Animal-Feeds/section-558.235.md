##### § 558.235 Efrotomycin. #####

(a) *Specifications.* Type A medicated articles containing 14.5 grams efrotomycin per pound.

(b) *Sponsor.* See No. 000010 in § 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) *Related tolerances.* See § 556.224 of this chapter.

(d) *Conditions of use in swine*—

|Efrotomycin  <br/>in grams/ton|          Indications for use           |                                      Limitations                                      |Sponsor|
|------------------------------|----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------|
|           (1) 3.6            |  Swine: For improved feed efficiency   |Feed continuously as sole ration. Not to be used in swine weighing more than 250 pounds|000010 |
|       (2) 3.6 to 14.5        |Swine: For increased rate of weight gain|Feed continuously as sole ration. Not to be used in swine weighing more than 250 pounds|000010 |
