##### § 558.680 Zoalene. #####

(a) *Specifications.* Type A medicated article containing 25 percent zoalene.

(b) *Approvals.* See No. 054771 in § 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) *Related tolerances.* See § 556.770 of this chapter.
